## 1. US Military Conducts Self-Defense Strikes in Iran 

The US military reported that it carried out **self-defense** strikes in Iran. These strikes targeted missile launch sites. The military said the actions were necessary to protect American forces and interests. This comes after increased tensions between the US and Iran. The strikes could lead to more conflict in the region. It is important because it shows how quickly military situations can change. We may see more actions or talks between the two countries in the near future.

## 2. Trump Immigration Policy May Force Visa Holders to Leave US Before Applying for Green Cards 

A new immigration rule from the Trump administration could change how some visa holders apply for permanent residence or green cards. Specifically, many people in Georgia might have to leave the US before they can apply for a green card. This is a big change because before, people could apply while staying in the country. The rule means applicants must apply from their home country or another country outside the US. This could cause problems and delays for many immigrants. Some experts worry this will make the immigration process more difficult for families.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **visa holder** — 비자 소지자  
>   _A **visa holder** must follow the rules of their visa._  
> - **green card** — 영주권  
>   _Many immigrants want to get a **green card** to live permanently._  
> - **apply for** — 신청하다  
>   _You need to **apply for** a visa before traveling._  
> - **cause problems** — 문제를 일으키다  
>   _The new rule could **cause problems** for many applicants._

## 3. US Teens Face the Toughest Summer Job Market in 78 Years 

This summer is the hardest job market for American teenagers in nearly 80 years. Many teens want summer jobs, but there are fewer openings than before. The job market is very **competitive**, which means many people are trying for the same jobs. This makes it hard for teens to find work. Some employers are offering fewer positions because of economic challenges. Having a summer job is important for teens to gain experience and earn money. But this year, many will have to try harder or wait for better opportunities.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 취업 시장  
>   _The **job market** is very competitive this year._  
> - **competitive** — 경쟁이 치열한  
>   _Finding a job is hard because it is **competitive**._  
> - **gain experience** — 경험을 쌓다  
>   _Summer jobs help teens **gain experience**._  
> - **offer positions** — 일자리를 제공하다  
>   _Some companies **offer positions** only to a few applicants._

## 4. How Silicon Valley and Private Finance Are Changing War and AI Policy 

Silicon Valley, known for technology companies, is playing a bigger role in war and artificial intelligence (AI) policies. Private finance, which means money from private investors, is helping fund new tech for military use. This changes how wars are fought and how governments think about technology. Recently, Silicon Valley also influenced the blocking of a Trump order on AI. This shows that tech companies have power not only in business but in politics too. The growth of AI creates new challenges and questions about safety and ethics.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **artificial intelligence (AI)** — 인공지능  
>   _Many companies are working on **artificial intelligence** technology._  
> - **private finance** — 민간 자금  
>   _**Private finance** funds many new tech projects._  
> - **influence** — 영향을 미치다  
>   _Tech companies can **influence** government decisions._  
> - **challenge** — 도전, 문제  
>   _AI brings new **challenges** for society._
