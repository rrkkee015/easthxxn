## 1. No Deal After Long Talks on Hostage Release and Diplomacy  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-12-1.png)

The U.S. held a very long meeting with officials from Iran and Pakistan to discuss important issues, including hostage releases and diplomacy. After many hours of **direct talks**, no agreement was made. These talks are part of ongoing efforts to improve relations and find peaceful solutions. The failure to reach a deal means the situation remains uncertain, and more discussions will likely follow. This is important because it affects international safety and future cooperation. Both sides said they want to keep working but face many challenges. People around the world are watching closely to see what will happen next.

## 2. Trump’s Immigration Policy Affects Foreign Doctors During Shortage  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-12-2.png)

The United States is facing a shortage of doctors, but recent immigration policies have made it harder for foreign doctors to work in the country. The Trump administration’s rules have **sideline**d many skilled foreign medical workers. This means fewer doctors are available to help patients, especially in hospitals that need more staff. The shortage is a problem for healthcare across the country. Many experts worry that these policies could make the situation worse. The government might need to change the rules to allow more foreign doctors to work here and help solve the shortage.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **sideline** — 무시하다, 배제하다  
>   _The new rules **sidelined** many foreign experts._  
> - **shortage** — 부족, 결핍  
>   _There is a **shortage** of nurses in the hospital._  
> - **skilled** — 숙련된, 능숙한  
>   _The company needs more **skilled** workers._  
> - **affect** — 영향을 미치다  
>   _The weather can **affect** our travel plans._

## 3. U.S. Job Openings and Hiring Fall in February  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-12-3.png)

In February, the number of job openings and new hires in the U.S. dropped. This means fewer companies are looking for workers, and fewer people got hired. The **job market** is showing signs of slowing down after a strong period. Experts say this could be a normal change or a sign of economic challenges ahead. Workers might find it a bit harder to get new jobs soon. Still, some industries are doing better than others. It will be important to watch future reports to understand the overall trend in jobs and hiring.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 취업 시장  
>   _The **job market** is competitive this year._  
> - **drop** — 떨어지다, 감소하다

> - **signs of** — ~의 징후

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces Pressure Over Taiwan Tech Supply  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-12-4.png)

Silicon Valley, a center for technology and innovation, is feeling pressure about its relationship with Taiwan. Taiwan makes many important parts for computers and electronics. The U.S. and tech companies are worried they may be running out of time to prepare for any problems with Taiwan’s supply. This is because Taiwan is a key player in the global tech chain. If there are issues, it could affect many products and the economy. Silicon Valley is trying to find ways to support Taiwan and keep supplies safe. This situation shows how technology and politics are connected today.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **running out of time** — 시간이 부족하다, 거의 다 되다  
>   _We are **running out of time** to finish the project._  
> - **pressure** — 압박, 부담  
>   _The team feels **pressure** before the big game._  
> - **key player** — 중요한 역할을 하는 사람/기관  
>   _She is a **key player** in the company’s success._  
> - **tech chain** — 기술 공급망  
>   _The **tech chain** depends on many countries._
