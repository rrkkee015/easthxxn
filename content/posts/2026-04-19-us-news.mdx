## 1. Strait of Hormuz Closed After Attacks on Ships ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-19-1.png)

The Strait of Hormuz is an important waterway where many oil tankers pass. Recently, Iran said the strait is **closed again** after some ships were attacked. This news worries many countries because the strait is a key route for global oil supply. When the strait is closed or blocked, oil prices usually go up. It is not clear how long the closure will last or if the attacks will continue. Many governments are watching this situation closely because it can affect world energy and trade. The tension shows how fragile peace is in this part of the world.

## 2. More Than Half of Americans Say Trump’s Immigration Policy Is Too Aggressive ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-19-2.png)

A recent poll shows that more than half of Americans believe former President Trump’s immigration policy was **too aggressive**. This means many people think the policy was too strict or harsh. Immigration is a big topic in the US, and opinions often vary widely. Some people want stronger borders, while others support more help for immigrants. The poll shows that the country is still divided on this issue. Understanding these opinions is important because immigration affects many parts of society, including jobs and communities. Leaders may need to find a balance that most people can agree on.

## 3. U.S. Employers Added 178,000 Jobs in March, Showing Strong Growth ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-19-3.png)

In March, U.S. employers hired 178,000 new workers. This number is higher than expected and shows a **strong rebound** from slower job growth in February. The job market is important because it shows how well the economy is doing. When more people have jobs, they can spend money, and businesses grow. However, some experts say the hiring boom might not last long. There are still concerns about the economy and future job opportunities. Watching these trends helps people understand what might happen next in the job market.

## 4. AI Is Changing Silicon Valley and Its Future ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-19-4.png)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is changing many parts of the world, especially Silicon Valley, the center of technology in California. AI tools are helping companies work faster and create new products. But this change also brings challenges. Some people worry about jobs being lost or the technology being used in the wrong way. Silicon Valley is trying to find ways to use AI for good while managing risks. This technology could change the world, but first, it is changing how Silicon Valley works every day.
