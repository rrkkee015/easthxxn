## 1. Rising Tensions: US Soldiers Killed in Iranian Strike ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-04-1.png)

Recently, six US soldiers died in an attack on a military base in Kuwait. The strike was carried out by Iranian forces, increasing tensions in the Middle East. This event is serious because it may lead to more conflict between the US and Iran. The US government is watching the situation closely and may respond with **escalated** strikes. This means that future attacks could be stronger or more frequent. The news shows how quickly international relations can change. Many people hope for peace, but the situation remains uncertain.

## 2. Fewer Au Pairs Are Coming to the US ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-03-04-2.png)

The number of au pairs coming to the US has dropped recently. Some experts think this is because of strict **immigration policies**. These rules make it harder for young people from other countries to enter the US as au pairs. Au pairs live with a family and help take care of children while learning about American culture. Many families now find it difficult to find au pairs. This change affects both the families and the young workers. If the policy does not change, the number of au pairs may continue to fall.

> - **immigration policies** — 이민 정책

## 3. More Americans Are Holding on Tight to Their Jobs ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-04-3.png)

Many workers in the US are choosing to stay in their current jobs. This is called “job retention,” and it has increased recently. One reason is that people want job security after uncertain times in the economy. Also, some are waiting for better opportunities or higher pay before changing jobs. Employers are working harder to keep good workers by offering better benefits or work conditions. This trend shows how important a stable **job market** is for many Americans. It might also affect how companies hire new employees in the future.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job retention** — 직장 유지, 일자리 유지  
>   _Company programs can improve **job retention** rates._  
> - **job security** — 직업 안정성  
>   _Workers value **job security** during economic uncertainty._  
> - **job market** — 구직 시장  
>   _The **job market** is competitive for new graduates._  
> - **employer** — 고용주

## 4. Silicon Valley Supports Anthropic in AI Fight Against Trump ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-04-4.png)

In the tech world, Silicon Valley is backing a company called Anthropic in its dispute with former President Trump. Anthropic works in artificial intelligence (AI), which is technology that can learn and think like humans. The fight is about government rules and policies that affect AI companies. Many tech leaders believe that these policies could slow down innovation. Silicon Valley’s support shows how important AI is for the future economy and technology. This battle may change how AI is developed and regulated in the US.
