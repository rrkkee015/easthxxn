## 1. US Job Growth Slows Down in June After Strong Months 

The US added only 57,000 new jobs in June, much less than experts expected. This number shows that the **job market** is cooling down after a long period of strong growth. Many employers are now more careful about hiring because of worries about the economy. This slowdown could mean slower economic growth in the near future. Some workers may find it harder to get new jobs or raises. The government will watch these trends closely to decide on policies. The report shows employers are pulling back and being careful with new hires.

## 2. New Public Charge Rule Changes Immigration Process 

The US government has introduced a new public charge rule that affects immigrants. This rule looks at whether immigrants might use public benefits like welfare or food assistance. If they do, it could make it harder for them to get a green card or visa. Supporters say this rule helps protect public resources. Critics say it makes life harder for many immigrants and their families. The rule could change how many people decide to apply for legal status. It is important because it affects millions of immigrants living in the US.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **public charge** — 공공부조 수혜자  
>   _The **public charge** rule checks if immigrants use government help._  
> - **green card** — 영주권  
>   _She applied for a **green card** to live permanently in the US._  
> - **critics say** — 비평가들은 말한다  
>   _**Critics say** the rule is unfair to immigrant families._  
> - **protect public resources** — 공공 자원을 보호하다  
>   _The government wants to **protect public resources** for citizens._

## 3. The Rising Cost of the Iran War Reaches $37.5 Billion 

The cost of the war related to Iran has reached an estimated $37.5 billion. This information was shared by a political commentator. These costs include military spending, equipment, and support for troops. The high cost shows how expensive war can be for the US government and taxpayers. It also raises questions about how long the conflict will last and what the final impact will be. Many Americans are concerned about where this money comes from and how it affects other services. The government may face pressure to explain or reduce spending.

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces New Challenges as China Advances in AI 

Silicon Valley’s lead in artificial intelligence (AI) is being challenged by China’s rapid progress. Tech companies in the US worry that China is quickly catching up and may pass them soon. This race is important because AI will change many industries and jobs. The competition also has political and economic effects between the two countries. Some experts say Silicon Valley must innovate faster and work with the government to stay ahead. The future of AI will impact not only technology but also global power.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **faces new challenges** — 새로운 도전에 직면하다  
>   _Silicon Valley **faces new challenges** from global competitors._  
> - **catching up** — 따라잡다  
>   _China is **catching up** in AI technology._  
> - **innovate faster** — 더 빨리 혁신하다  
>   _Companies must **innovate faster** to stay competitive._  
> - **stay ahead** — 앞서 나가다  
>   _Silicon Valley wants to **stay ahead** in AI research._
