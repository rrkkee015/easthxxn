## 1. New Clues in Nancy Guthrie’s Disappearance ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-02-11-1.png)

Nancy Guthrie has been missing, and police recently released surveillance video to the public. After sharing the video, they **detained** a person for questioning. This means the police stopped someone to ask questions about the case. The police want to find out if this person knows anything about Nancy’s disappearance. The case is important because it has worried many people in the community. The police are still working hard to find answers and hope the public’s help will **shed light** on what happened. This means they want to make the situation clearer and understand it better.

## 2. Appeals Court Supports Trump’s Immigration Detention Policy ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-02-11-2.png)

A U.S. appeals court recently **upheld** a strict immigration detention policy from the Trump administration. This policy requires many immigrants to stay in detention centers while their cases are decided. Supporters say this helps keep the country safe. But critics say it is too harsh and hurts families. The court’s decision means the policy will continue for now. This case is important because it affects many immigrants and the rules for how the U.S. manages immigration. The debate will likely continue as new policies are discussed.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **uphold** — 지지하다, 유지하다  
>   _The court **upheld** the law after careful review._  
> - **detention policy** — 구금 정책  
>   _The **detention policy** affects how immigrants are held._  
> - **critics** — 비평가들, 반대하는 사람들

Experts will watch the report closely to understand if the U.S. economy is still strong or facing challenges. This report may influence government decisions about jobs and money.
