## 1. Rising Tensions: US Strikes Iran Amid Strong Words 

The United States has launched another round of military **strikes** against Iran. This action comes during a time when former President Trump and Iran’s supreme leader are exchanging strong threats. The situation is serious and could lead to more conflicts in the Middle East. These strikes are part of ongoing tensions between the two countries, which have a long history of disagreements. Many experts worry this could affect global peace and oil prices. The US government says it is acting to protect its interests and allies. It is important to watch how Iran will respond in the coming days.

## 2. Immigration Reform: The DIGNITY Act Gains Attention 

The DIGNITY Act is a new immigration reform bill that has been getting a lot of news coverage. More than 250 stories have talked about this bill across the country. The act aims to change how immigration laws work and to protect immigrants’ rights. Many lawmakers support the bill because it could bring fairness and respect to people who want to live in the US legally. However, some groups still have concerns about the bill’s details. The national conversation about immigration is growing, and this bill is an important part of that. It shows that immigration reform is a key topic in America today.

## 3. Hiring Slows Down: US Employers Pull Back in June 

In June, US employers hired fewer workers than expected. This means companies are being careful about adding new jobs right now. Many factors, like economic uncertainty and higher costs, may be causing this slowdown. The White House says the labor market still shows signs of growth, but the pace is slower. Hiring is important because it affects how many people have work and how the economy grows. If this trend continues, it could mean fewer job opportunities for workers soon. Experts will watch the job market closely in the next months to see if hiring picks up again.

## 4. Silicon Valley’s New Direction: The Rise of the “Right” 

Silicon Valley, known for its tech innovation, is seeing a political change. More people in the tech industry are starting to support conservative ideas. This shift is called the rise of the “Silicon Valley Right.” Many tech workers and leaders are rethinking their political views because of new challenges like regulations and free speech. This change could affect how tech companies work and their role in society. Some believe this new direction will bring more balance, while others worry it may cause conflicts. The tech world is watching closely how these political changes will shape the future.

> ### 영어 표현 학습

> - **rethink political views** — 정치적 견해를 재고하다
