## 1. Trump Calls Iran’s Response ‘Totally Unacceptable’ to US Peace Proposal  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-05-11-1.png)

Former President Trump strongly criticized Iran’s reply to a US peace offer. He called Iran’s answer **“totally unacceptable”** and said the US will not accept their terms. This news comes as tensions between the two countries remain high. The US wants peace talks, but Iran’s response shows they are not ready to agree. Many experts worry this could slow down efforts for peace in the region. The situation is important because it affects global security and US foreign policy. We will have to wait and see if there will be new talks or more conflict.

## 2. US Jobs Market Shows Strong Growth for Second Month  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-05-11-2.png)

## 5. American Companies Push the Idea of an AI Race with China  
![Article 5](/images/news/2026-05-11-5.png)

Many American companies are promoting the idea that the US is in an **AI race** with China. They say it is important to develop artificial intelligence faster than China to stay competitive. This message is used to encourage more investment and government support for AI research. However, some experts warn this race idea can cause unnecessary tensions and pressure. AI technology is changing quickly, and countries want to be leaders in this field. Watching how this race develops will show how AI will impact the future of technology and global relations.
