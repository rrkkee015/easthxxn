## 1. Political Candidate Suspends Campaign After Serious Accusation  
  

A political candidate in Maine has **suspended** their bid for the Senate after facing a serious accusation of rape. This means the candidate is stopping their campaign for now. Such accusations are very serious and can affect public trust and votes. The candidate has not denied the claim but decided to pause their race to avoid distractions. This news is important because it shows how personal behavior can impact political careers. People are watching how the legal process will move forward. The outcome could change the political landscape in Maine.

## 2. National Debate Grows Around Immigration Reform Act  
  

More than 250 news stories recently covered the DIGNITY Act, a new immigration reform bill in the US. This act aims to change how the country handles immigration, especially for people trying to live and work in the US legally. The bill is part of a larger national conversation about fair treatment and legal rights for immigrants. Many people support it because they want more dignity and respect for immigrants. Others have concerns about how it might affect border security. Events and discussions are planned to help people understand the bill better and ask questions. The future of this law depends on what lawmakers decide next.

## 3. U.S. Job Market Shows Stability but Growth Slows  
  

In May and June, the US job market stayed mostly stable, but job growth has slowed down compared to earlier months. This means there are still many job openings, but fewer new jobs are being created. The hospitality sector, like hotels and restaurants, lost some roles even while big sports events were taking place. Experts say this slowing growth is normal as the economy adjusts after strong hiring last year. Workers and employers are watching the job market carefully because it affects income and spending. How the job market changes next will influence the US economy and people's daily lives.

## 4. The Secret AI Competition Between Silicon Valley and China  
  

There is a secretive and intense competition in artificial intelligence (AI) technology between Silicon Valley in the US and companies in China. Both sides want to be the leader in AI, which is a type of computer technology that can learn and solve problems. This race is important because AI will affect many parts of life and business in the future. The competition involves not just companies, but also governments supporting their tech industries. Sometimes, this battle is called a "war" because of how serious it is. Experts say this AI race could change global power and technology leadership.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **artificial intelligence (AI)** — 인공지능  
>   _Many companies invest in **artificial intelligence (AI)** to improve products._  
> - **intense competition** — 치열한 경쟁  
>   _There is **intense competition** in the smartphone market._  
> - **tech industry** — 기술 산업  
>   _The **tech industry** is growing rapidly._  
> - **leader in** — ~의 선두 주자  
>   _The company is a **leader in** renewable energy._  
> - **global power** — 세계 강대국  
>   _Technology plays a role in becoming a **global power**._
