## 1. Israel-Lebanon Ceasefire Extended After Peace Talks  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-24-1.png)  

The United States helped extend the ceasefire between Israel and Lebanon for another three weeks. This happened after talks at the White House involving leaders from both sides. The ceasefire means that both countries agreed to stop fighting for now. This is important because it helps reduce violence in the region and gives time for longer peace talks. People hope this will lead to a more permanent peace agreement. The extension also shows that the U.S. is playing a role as a peace **broker** in international conflicts. We will have to wait and see if both sides keep to the ceasefire or if fighting starts again.

## 2. New Limits on Legal Immigration Could Slow U.S. Population Growth  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-24-2.png)  

The U.S. government under former President Trump put new rules on legal immigration. These rules make it harder for people to move to the United States legally. Experts say this could **sharply reduce** population growth in the U.S. in the future. Population growth is important because it affects the economy, schools, and jobs. If fewer immigrants come, the U.S. might have fewer workers and less economic growth. Some people support the rules to control immigration, but others worry about their long-term effects. The government may change these rules again depending on future policies.

## 3. Nevada Leads U.S. in Job Growth for 2025  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-24-3.png)  

Nevada is the leading state in the U.S. for job growth in 2025. This means more new jobs were created there than in any other state. The job market in Nevada is growing because of new businesses and industries moving in. This is good news for people looking for work. The growth helps the local economy and can bring more money to communities. Other states are also seeing job increases, but Nevada is ahead. Experts will watch to see if Nevada keeps this strong job market in the coming years.

## 4. How A.I. Is Changing Silicon Valley  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-24-4.png)  

Artificial Intelligence (A.I.) is changing the way Silicon Valley works. Many companies are using A.I. to create new products and improve old ones. This technology can do tasks faster and sometimes better than humans. Because of A.I., some jobs are changing or disappearing. A.I. also brings new opportunities for work and business. Experts say Silicon Valley faces both challenges and chances because of this change. People are watching closely to see how A.I. will affect the technology industry and the economy.
