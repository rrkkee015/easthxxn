## 1. Trump Celebrates America’s 250th Birthday with a Bold Speech  


On July 4, 2026, former President Donald Trump gave a speech to celebrate America’s 250th birthday. In his address, he said, “**Nobody can be like us**,” showing pride in American history and strength. Trump talked about the country’s achievements and the importance of freedom. His speech was a mix of hope and confidence, aimed at inspiring people to believe in America’s future. Many people listened carefully because this day is a big milestone for the United States. The event included fireworks and celebrations across the country. Trump's message may influence political discussions as the nation looks ahead.

## 2. ICE Arrests Surge with Over 10,000 Immigrants Detained in 5 Days  


U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) recently increased arrests and detained more than 10,000 immigrants in just five days. This is part of a new plan to enforce immigration laws more strictly. The effort focuses on people who ICE says have broken immigration rules or have criminal records. Many immigrant communities are worried about the sudden rise in arrests. Officials say this crackdown is necessary to keep the country safe and enforce the law. However, critics argue it causes fear and confusion among families. The government’s immigration policy may continue to change in the coming months.

## 3. US Job Growth Slows Sharply in June, Adding Only 57,000 Jobs  


In June 2026, the U.S. job market slowed down a lot. Only 57,000 new jobs were created, much fewer than experts expected. The unemployment rate stayed at 4.2%, which is still low but shows the economy might be changing. Many businesses are hiring less because of economic uncertainty and higher costs. The slow job growth may affect wages and workers’ confidence. Experts will watch the next few months carefully to see if this is a short pause or a bigger problem. The government may decide to change policies to help the job market.

## 4. Tech Industry Faces Confusion Over Trump’s Changing AI Policies  


The U.S. tech industry is having a hard time with sudden changes in government AI policies. Former President Trump has made several **about-faces**, meaning he changed his position quickly or completely. These changes make it difficult for companies to plan their AI projects. Many tech leaders want clear and stable rules to help their businesses grow safely. AI is a fast-moving field, and policy confusion can slow down innovation. The government is trying to balance safety with progress, but the tech world hopes for more consistent decisions soon.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **about-face** — 갑작스러운 입장 변화  
>   _The company made an **about-face** on its product strategy._  
> - **grapple with** — (문제 등을) 힘겹게 다루다  
>   _Schools are **grappling with** new technology challenges._  
> - **policy confusion** — 정책 혼란  
>   _The new rules caused **policy confusion** among businesses._  
> - **innovation** — 혁신  
>   _The tech sector depends on continuous **innovation**._
