## 1. U.S. Starts New Talks with Iran About Nuclear Program  
  

The United States has delayed a planned trip to Switzerland. This delay is so a top official, Vance, can lead new talks with Iran about its nuclear program. These talks are very important because many countries want to stop Iran from building dangerous nuclear weapons. The U.S. wants to find a peaceful solution without starting a conflict. This meeting could help reduce tensions in the Middle East. If the talks **move forward** successfully, they might lead to new agreements that keep everyone safer. But if they fail, the situation might get worse, causing more problems worldwide.

## 2. A Quick Look at U.S. Immigration History and Trump-Era Changes  
  

The history of U.S. immigration policy helps us understand today's debates. Over many years, laws have changed to allow or limit who can enter the country. Recently, during the Trump administration, policies became stricter. These changes caused more people to feel worried or angry about immigration. Some believe these strict rules were unfair and hurt families. This article explains how past laws and ideas about protecting the country led to today's tough immigration rules. Understanding this **background** helps us see why immigration is still a big topic in America. In the future, new policies might change again depending on the government.

## 3. U.S. Job Market Shows Strong Growth Despite Challenges  
  

The American job market is getting stronger again. In May, 172,000 new jobs were added, showing the economy is still healthy. Even with problems like the conflict involving Iran, the number of job openings in the U.S. reached 7.6 million in April. This means many companies want to hire workers. Experts say this growth is a sign of **market resilience**, which means the job market can handle problems and keep working well. However, some people worry about future challenges. For now, the job market looks hopeful for workers and businesses.

## 4. U.S. Limits Foreign Access to Advanced AI Models  
  

The United States government has made new rules to restrict foreign countries from using some advanced AI models. These models come from a company called Anthropic, which creates smart artificial intelligence technologies. The goal is to protect U.S. technology and keep control over powerful AI tools. This decision has caused some worry in Silicon Valley, where many tech companies work with international partners. The new rules show how important AI technology has become for national security. Experts say these limits might slow down global cooperation in AI research. The future may see more rules like this as countries try to protect their technology.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **restrict access** — 접근을 제한하다  
>   _The company decided to **restrict access** to its new software._  
> - **advanced AI models** — 고급 인공지능 모델  
>   _Researchers are developing **advanced AI models** for language._  
> - **national security** — 국가 안보  
>   _Protecting data is important for **national security**._  
> - **Silicon Valley** — 실리콘 밸리 (미국 IT 산업 중심지)  
>   _Many tech startups are based in **Silicon Valley**._
