## 1. U.S. Navy Secretary Fired by Pete Hegseth ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-23-1.png)

Pete Hegseth, a U.S. official, has **fired** the Navy Secretary John Phelan. This decision surprised many because the Navy Secretary is an important leader in the military. The firing comes during a time when the Navy faces many challenges. The Navy Secretary helps manage Navy operations and resources, so this change could affect how the Navy works. Some experts say this move might cause **uncertainty** in the Navy's leadership. It is not clear what will happen next or who will replace Phelan.
