## 1. Strong Job Market Shows Positive Signs in the US  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-16-1.png)

The latest report shows the US job market is doing well. More people are getting hired, and many jobs are available. This is important because a strong **job market** means the economy is healthy. When more people work, they can spend money and help businesses grow. However, some experts warn that job openings and hiring dropped in February, so the situation might change. In Colorado, job openings are also showing interesting trends that could affect the local economy. Overall, the good news gives hope for workers and employers alike.

## 2. Houston Faces Conflict Over Immigration Rules  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-16-2.png)

Houston, Texas, is having a serious fight with the state government about immigration laws. The city wants to pass a new **ordinance** to protect immigrants, but the state opposes it. This is important because immigration rules affect many families and schools in the area. Some believe strict immigration enforcement is hurting students and schools across the US. When students feel unsafe, it can stop them from learning well. The conflict in Houston shows how immigration is still a big issue in American cities. What happens next could change life for many people.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **ordinance** — 조례, 법규  
>   _The city passed a new **ordinance** to improve public safety._  
> - **immigration enforcement** — 이민 단속  
>   _**Immigration enforcement** has increased near the border._  
> - **conflict** — 갈등, 충돌  
>   _There is a **conflict** between the city and state about the law._

## 3. Iran Threatens Major US Tech Companies  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-16-3.png)

Iran has warned big US tech companies like Meta, Google, and Apple. The country said, "**From now on**," there will be new actions against these companies because of political conflicts. This is serious because these tech companies play a big role in daily life and business. If Iran blocks or limits their work, it could affect many users and the global tech market. This threat adds tension to already difficult relations between Iran and the US. People are watching closely to see what happens next.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **From now on** — 지금부터, 앞으로  
>   _**From now on**, we will have new rules to follow._  
> - **warned** — 경고했다  
>   _The government **warned** the company about safety issues._  
> - **major** — 주요한, 큰  
>   _Google is a **major** player in the tech world._

## 4. Silicon Valley Supports AI Company Anthropic  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-16-4.png)

Silicon Valley, the center of US technology, is quietly supporting Anthropic, a company working on artificial intelligence (AI). Many big tech leaders are backing this company because AI is becoming more important every day. Anthropic aims to create safe and useful AI tools that can help people and businesses. This support shows how the tech world is focusing on AI's future. However, some critics say Silicon Valley has not done enough for most Americans outside the tech industry. Watching Anthropic's progress will be important for understanding AI's impact.
