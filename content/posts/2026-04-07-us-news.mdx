## 1. Iran Peace Talks Face a Critical Deadline ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-07-1.png)

The United States has set a **deadline** for Iran to agree to a peace deal by tomorrow. If Iran does not accept the terms, President Trump warned that the **entire country** could face serious consequences. Iran has sent a **maximalist** peace plan, which means they want very strong demands in return. This situation is very tense because the talks have been going on for a long time without an agreement. Many people are watching closely because the outcome could affect global peace and security. If no deal is reached, the US might take stronger actions. This moment shows how important diplomacy and negotiation are in world politics.

## 2. Trump’s Immigration Policy Affects Foreign Doctors ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-07-2.png)

The US is facing a shortage of doctors, but new immigration policies are making it harder for foreign doctors to work in the country. Many hospitals depend on these doctors, especially in rural or busy areas. The policy changes have **sidelined** foreign doctors, meaning they are pushed aside or delayed in getting permission to work. This problem may cause bigger issues in health care because there are not enough doctors to treat patients. Some experts say the policy is unfair and could harm the health system. The government may need to rethink its approach to balance safety and the need for medical professionals.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **shortage** — 부족, 결핍  
>   _There is a **shortage** of nurses in many hospitals._  
> - **sideline** — (중요한 일에서) 제외하다, 밀쳐 놓다  
>   _The new rules **sidelined** many skilled workers._  
> - **policy** — 정책  
>   _The government introduced a new **policy** to improve education._

## 3. Job Market Sees Surprising Growth Despite Challenges ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-07-3.png)

In March, the United States added 178,000 new jobs, which was more than many experts expected. This growth shows the job market is still strong, even with some economic worries. However, some industries are changing fast because of technology. For example, AI is cutting 16,000 jobs a month, especially affecting young workers in Generation Z. This shows a mix of good news and challenges in the employment world. Workers may need new skills to keep up with these changes. The job market is **defying expectations** by growing, but it is also facing new pressures.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 일자리 시장, 고용시장  
>   _The **job market** is competitive this year._  
> - **defy expectations** — 기대에 반하다, 예상과 다르게 행동하다  
>   _The team’s success **defied expectations**._  
> - **cut jobs** — 일자리를 없애다, 감원하다  
>   _Companies are **cutting jobs** to save money._

## 4. Silicon Valley’s Political Shift: Power Over Ideology? ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-07-4.png)

Silicon Valley, known as a center for technology and innovation, may be changing its political focus. Some experts say it is moving away from traditional left-leaning ideas and focusing more on **power** and influence. This means companies and leaders want to gain more control and impact, no matter their political side. The shift could affect how tech companies act in politics and business. Silicon Valley’s role is important because it shapes much of the technology used worldwide. Watching this change helps us understand future trends in tech and society.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **political shift** — 정치적 변화  
>   _There has been a **political shift** in the country recently._  
> - **power** — 권력, 영향력  
>   _The company gained more **power** in the market._  
> - **influence** — 영향력  
>   _Social media has a strong **influence** on young people._
