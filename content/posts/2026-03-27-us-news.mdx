## 1. Trump Orders TSA Agents to Be Paid Despite Funding Delays  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-27-1.png)

Former President Trump said he will **order** the Transportation Security Administration (TSA) agents to be paid even though the government funding deal is stuck. Normally, if funding stops, some workers might not get paid on time. TSA agents are important because they keep airports safe by checking travelers. If they are not paid, it could cause delays and problems at airports. Trump’s order shows he wants to make sure these workers are not **left in the dark** about their pay. This move is unusual because usually Congress must approve payments. It is not clear how this will work legally or if it will cause more disagreements in Washington. Many people are watching to see what happens next with the funding deal.

> - **funding deal** — 자금 조달 합의  
>   _Congress must approve the **funding deal** for government projects._

## 2. US Immigration Policies Affect Transit Countries  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-03-27-2.png)

The United States has immigration policies that not only affect people coming directly to the US but also impact countries where migrants travel through. These “transit countries” are places where migrants stay for some time before reaching the US. New research shows that US policies can change the flow of migrants in these countries. Sometimes stricter rules in the US cause more migrants to stay longer in transit countries, which can create challenges for those countries. This is important because it affects many people who want a better life and the countries that help them. Understanding these effects can help make better immigration policies in the future.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **transit countries** — 경유 국가  
>   _Many migrants pass through **transit countries** before reaching their final destination._  
> - **immigration policies** — 이민 정책  
>   _Changes in **immigration policies** can affect migration patterns._  
> - **flow of migrants** — 이주민의 이동 흐름  
>   _The **flow of migrants** increased after the new rules were introduced._

## 3. Job Market Worries Grow as Worker Confidence Declines  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-27-3.png)

Recent reports show that fewer American workers feel they are **thriving** in their jobs. Many people are worried about the future of the job market. When workers feel uncertain, it can affect how they work and spend money. The report says this feeling of **pessimism** is growing, meaning more people expect bad things to happen in the job market. Young graduates face especially hard times finding good jobs. Experts say this could slow down the economy if it continues. It is important for the government and businesses to find ways to improve workers’ feelings and opportunities.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **thriving** — 번창하는, 잘 나가는  
>   _The local business is **thriving** despite the tough economy._  
> - **pessimism** — 비관주의  
>   _There is growing **pessimism** about job opportunities this year._  
> - **job market** — 취업 시장  
>   _The **job market** can be very competitive for new graduates._

## 4. US and China Differ on AI but Could Work Together  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-27-4.png)

A Silicon Valley tech founder said the United States and China have **disjointed** views on artificial intelligence (AI). This means their ideas and plans for AI do not match well. However, he also said there is still a chance for **collaboration** between the two countries in AI development. AI is important because it can change many parts of life, like jobs, security, and technology. Both countries want to be leaders in AI but see risks and benefits differently. Experts hope that by working together, they can make AI safer and better for everyone. The future of AI depends a lot on how these two countries interact.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **disjointed** — 연결이 안 된, 일관성이 없는  
>   _Their opinions were **disjointed** and hard to understand._  
> - **collaboration** — 협력, 공동 작업  
>   _The project succeeded because of good **collaboration**._  
> - **artificial intelligence (AI)** — 인공 지능  
>   _Many companies use **artificial intelligence** to improve their services._
