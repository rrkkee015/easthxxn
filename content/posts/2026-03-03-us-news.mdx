## 1. Rising Tensions: Drones Hit U.S. Embassy in Saudi Arabia ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-03-1.png)

Recently, drones attacked the U.S. Embassy in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Saudi officials reported that multiple drones caused damage near the embassy. This incident adds to growing tensions between the U.S. and Iran, as some groups connected to Iran may be involved. The U.S. government is watching the situation closely because it could affect security in the Middle East. Such attacks can create fear and uncertainty in the region. Many experts think this could lead to more conflicts or stronger U.S. actions. Understanding these events is important because they might influence global politics and American safety abroad.

## 2. U.S. Immigration: Trump's Words vs. His Policies ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-03-03-2.png)

Former President Trump says he supports legal immigration. However, many people believe his immigration policies tell a different story. His government made it harder for many immigrants to enter or stay in the U.S. Critics say these rules do not match his words. Immigration is an important topic because it affects millions of people and the economy. Some legal experts and community leaders are watching closely to see how these policies change in the future. Understanding the difference between what politicians say and what they do helps people make informed decisions.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **tell a different story** — 다른 이야기를 하다, 말과 행동이 다르다

## 3. Job Market Trends: More Employees Holding On to Their Jobs ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-03-3.png)

Many workers in the U.S. are keeping their jobs longer than before. This means fewer people are quitting or changing jobs. Experts say this happens because workers want to feel safe and secure, especially after economic problems in recent years. Some companies are also offering better benefits to keep employees. Holding on to a job can help people feel more stable in their lives. However, it might also slow down new hiring. The job market is always changing, so it is good to understand why people behave this way.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **holding on to their jobs** — 직장을 계속 유지하다  
>   _More employees are **holding on to their jobs** during uncertain times._  
> - **feel safe and secure** — 안전하고 안정감을 느끼다  
>   _Workers want to **feel safe and secure** in their positions._  
> - **offering better benefits** — 더 좋은 복지를 제공하다  
>   _Companies are **offering better benefits** to keep their staff._  
> - **job market** — 노동 시장, 구직 시장  
>   _The **job market** changes depending on the economy._

## 4. Silicon Valley's A.I. Battle: Support for Anthropic Against Trump ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-03-4.png)

Silicon Valley companies are supporting Anthropic, an artificial intelligence (A.I.) firm, in a conflict with former President Trump. Anthropic focuses on creating safe and ethical A.I. technology. The Pentagon recently criticized the company, which surprised many tech leaders. This situation shows how important A.I. is becoming in politics and business. It also raises questions about government influence on technology companies. The future of A.I. could change depending on how these conflicts are resolved.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **supporting** — 지지하다  
>   _Many companies are **supporting** Anthropic in this dispute._  
> - **focuses on** — ~에 집중하다  
>   _Anthropic **focuses on** safe and ethical A.I. development._  
> - **raises questions** — 의문을 제기하다

> - **influence on** — ~에 대한 영향력
