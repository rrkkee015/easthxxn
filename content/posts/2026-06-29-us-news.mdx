## 1. U.S. and Iran Agree to Pause Military Strikes and Meet This Week  


The U.S. and Iran have agreed to **halt strikes** against each other and plan to meet this week to discuss their conflicts. This is important because it can reduce the risk of war in the Middle East. Recently, tensions were high after attacks and threats between the countries. By agreeing to stop strikes, both sides hope to find a peaceful solution through talks. This pause can help many countries avoid serious damage and loss. It is not clear yet what will happen in the meetings, but this step shows both sides want to try diplomacy. People around the world are watching closely because the results could affect global peace and oil prices.

## 2. Supreme Court Supports Revival of Strict Immigration Policy  


The U.S. Supreme Court has allowed the government to restart a strict immigration policy. This policy makes it harder for some immigrants to stay in the U.S. The policy was part of the Trump administration's efforts to control immigration. Supporters say it protects the country’s borders and security. Critics believe it is unfair and hurts many families. This decision means the government can continue using this policy while legal challenges continue. It shows how immigration remains a big and difficult topic in the U.S. The next steps could affect many immigrants and how the country controls its borders.

## 3. SpaceX Stock Drops, But Hiring in Space Jobs Grows  


SpaceX’s stock price has gone down recently, but the company is still hiring many workers. Jobs in the space economy are growing even though the stock market shows less interest. SpaceX wants to build more rockets and satellites, so it needs engineers and other workers. This shows companies can grow hiring even when their stock price falls. The space industry is becoming important for new technology and business. Many people want to work in this exciting field. The hiring trend may continue as space exploration and travel develop.

## 4. Tech Industry Faces Challenges with Changing AI Policies  


The tech industry is dealing with new and changing ideas about artificial intelligence (AI). Recently, some leaders have changed their opinions about AI rules and controls. This is causing uncertainty for tech companies that use AI in products and services. Many workers and investors want clear rules to help them plan for the future. The government and companies are trying to find the best way to support AI while keeping people safe. How this situation develops will affect many jobs and new technologies. It is an important moment for the future of AI in the U.S.
