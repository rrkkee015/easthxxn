## 1. US and Iran Prepare for Important Nuclear Talks in Switzerland  
  

The United States and Iran are getting ready for important talks in Switzerland about nuclear weapons. Both countries have made different claims about the Strait of Hormuz, a crucial waterway for oil shipping. These talks are part of efforts to reduce tensions after years of conflict and disagreements. US officials, including envoy Vance, traveled to Switzerland to join the discussions. The goal is to find common ground and possibly restart an old nuclear agreement. This matters because peace in this region affects global security and oil prices. What happens next could change relations between the US and Iran.

## 2. New Hearing Opens on Abuse of US Immigration Policy  
  

A new hearing started in the US House of Representatives to look at how some people may misuse the country's immigration rules. Representative Gill opened the hearing to hear stories and gather facts about abuses in the system. Immigration policy in the US has a long and complicated history that affects many people today. Some argue that the current policies can be unfair or used wrongly. The hearing will help lawmakers decide if changes are needed. This is important because immigration affects families, the economy, and national security. We may see new laws or reforms after this hearing.

## 3. Many Employers Post Jobs but Do Not Hire Quickly  
  

Many companies in the US are posting job openings, but they are not hiring people as fast as expected. This creates a confusing job market where there are many jobs but fewer new hires. Experts say this happens because employers want to find the best workers or wait to see how the economy changes. Also, some workers may be cautious about changing jobs. The number of job openings is still high, which shows businesses want to grow. This situation may continue for a while and affects how easy it is to find a new job. Job seekers should be patient and keep improving their skills.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **post a job** — 일자리를 올리다, 공고하다  
>   _Companies **post jobs** online to find workers._  
> - **hire quickly** — 빨리 채용하다  
>   _Some companies do not **hire quickly**._  
> - **job market** — 구직 시장  
>   _The **job market** can be hard for some people._  
> - **be cautious about** — ~에 대해 조심하다  
>   _Workers **are cautious about** changing jobs._  
> - **keep improving** — 계속 향상시키다  
>   _Job seekers should **keep improving** their skills._
