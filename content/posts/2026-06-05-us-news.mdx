## 1. Senate Blocks Major Election Reform Bill in the US  


The US Senate recently voted to stop a big plan that would change how elections are run. This plan was supported by many Republicans but did not get enough votes to pass. The bill aimed to change rules around voting and elections in many states. Some people think these changes would make elections safer, but others worry they could make voting harder for some groups. This fight shows how important and **sweeping** election rules are in American politics. The result means the current election system will stay the same for now. Many expect the debate to continue in the future as the country prepares for upcoming elections.

## 2. New Green Card Policy: What You Need to Know  


The Trump administration introduced a new policy that changes how people get green cards to live and work in the US. This policy focuses on stricter rules for who can qualify. For example, it looks more closely at a person’s income and health. The government says this will protect American workers and reduce public costs. On the other hand, many immigrants and experts say the policy makes it **harder** for many people to stay legally in the US. This change is important because it affects thousands of families and workers. It also shows how immigration is a big topic in US politics today.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **green card** — 영주권  
>   _She applied for a **green card** to live in the US permanently._  
> - **to qualify** — 자격을 갖추다  
>   _You must **qualify** to receive the scholarship._  
> - **to protect workers** — 노동자를 보호하다  
>   _New laws are designed **to protect workers** from unfair conditions._  
> - **harder** — 더 어려운  
>   _After the new rule, it is **harder** to get a visa._

## 3. Tech Industry’s Big Win in California Elections  


In California, the tech industry spent millions of dollars during the primary elections. Their goal was to support candidates who favor technology and business growth. This spending helped many tech-friendly candidates win important positions. The tech industry is very powerful in California, especially in places like Silicon Valley. This influence can shape laws and policies about the economy, privacy, and innovation. Experts say this is an example of how money and business interests can change political results. It also shows the close connection between technology companies and government today.
