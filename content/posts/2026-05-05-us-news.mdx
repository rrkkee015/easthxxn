## 1. Rising Tensions in the Gulf Threaten U.S.-Iran Ceasefire  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-05-05-1.png)  

Recently, attacks in the Strait of Hormuz and the Gulf region have put the **fragile** U.S.-Iran ceasefire at risk. This area is very important because it is a major route for oil shipments. The ceasefire was an agreement to reduce conflict between the two countries. However, these new attacks may **undermine** the progress made so far. Experts warn that if fighting continues, it could lead to serious problems for global energy markets and increase political instability in the region. The U.S. government is watching the situation closely and may respond with diplomatic or military actions. Many hope for calm to return, but the situation remains uncertain.

## 2. New Immigration Laws in Oregon Could Change Lives  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-05-05-2.png)  

Oregon has passed eight new immigration laws that may affect many immigrants in the state. These laws focus on improving access to education, healthcare, and legal support for immigrants. Officials say the goal is to help immigrants feel safer and more included in their communities. Some laws also make it easier for immigrants to get work permits and protect them from unfair treatment at work. These changes come after years of debate about immigration policy in the U.S. Many immigrants and advocates are hopeful these laws will improve daily life. However, some people worry about the costs and how the laws will be enforced.

## 3. College Graduates Find Best Job Markets in These Cities  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-05-05-3.png)  

Many recent college graduates want to know where the best job opportunities are. A new report shows several cities are “hiring hot spots” where graduates find good jobs quickly. These cities have growing industries like technology, healthcare, and finance. Employers in these areas look for skilled young workers and offer competitive salaries. However, some graduates still face challenges like high living costs and strong competition. Experts say graduates should research local job markets and consider moving to places with more opportunities. This trend could change as the economy shifts in the coming months.

## 4. Silicon Valley’s Political Shift Changes the Tech World  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-05-05-4.png)  

Silicon Valley, known as the center of the tech industry, is seeing a new political movement called the “Silicon Valley Right.” This group is different from the usual tech leaders who often support liberal ideas. The rise of this movement is **reshaping** the tech landscape by changing how companies think about politics and business. Some believe this shift could cause new conflicts inside tech companies. Others say it shows that Silicon Valley is becoming more diverse in opinions. This change might affect how technology develops and how companies connect with their customers. Experts say we should watch closely to see what happens next.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **reshaping** — 재형성하다, 바꾸다  
>   _New ideas are **reshaping** the way companies work._  
> - **landscape** — (분야의) 상황, 환경

> - **movement** — 운동, 움직임

> - **diverse** — 다양한
