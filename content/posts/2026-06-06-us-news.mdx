## 1. California Governor Race Sees Xavier Becerra Move Forward  
  

Xavier Becerra is making progress in the race to become California’s next governor. He has gained support from many voters and is seen as a strong candidate. This election is important because California is the most populous state in the US and has a big impact on national politics. Becerra’s campaign focuses on issues like healthcare, climate change, and education. The race is still competitive, but his recent success shows he has a good chance to win. If he becomes governor, his policies could affect millions of people in California. Voters will watch closely as the campaign continues.

## 2. Federal Court Blocks Trump-Era Immigration Policies  
  

A federal court has canceled some immigration policies made during the Trump administration. These policies targeted immigrants from 39 countries and caused many problems. The court said these rules were unfair because they focused on people’s country of origin. This decision is very important for immigrants and their families. It means the government cannot treat people differently just because of where they come from. Many immigration advocates welcomed the ruling and hope it leads to better policies. The government may need to create new rules that are fairer and follow the law.

## 3. U.S. Adds 172,000 Jobs, Many in Service Industries  
  

The United States added 172,000 new jobs recently. Most of these jobs are in restaurants, bars, and hotels. This shows that the economy is improving, especially in areas where people work face-to-face with customers. Job growth in these industries helps many workers who lost jobs during the pandemic. However, some experts say the increase is smaller than expected, so the recovery is still slow. Many Americans are happy to see new opportunities but remain worried about rising prices and the cost of living. The government and businesses will watch job numbers closely to understand the economy’s health.

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces Challenges as Many Indian Engineers Leave  
  

Silicon Valley, the center of the US tech industry, is seeing many Indian engineers leave the country. About 15,000 left last year, and more may follow. These engineers are important because they help create new technology and keep companies competitive. Some say the US immigration rules and work environment are reasons for leaving. Others feel that opportunities in other countries are becoming better. This trend worries tech companies that depend on skilled workers. The future of Silicon Valley may change if it cannot keep its talented engineers.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **face challenges** — 어려움을 겪다  
>   _The company **faces challenges** in finding skilled workers._  
> - **leave the country** — 나라를 떠나다  
>   _Many workers **leave the country** to find better jobs._  
> - **keep competitive** — 경쟁력을 유지하다  
>   _Innovation helps companies **keep competitive** in the market._  
> - **depend on** — ~에 의존하다
