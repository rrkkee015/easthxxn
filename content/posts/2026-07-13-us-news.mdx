## 1. Senator Lindsey Graham’s Cause of Death Confirmed  
  

Senator Lindsey Graham recently passed away, and a preliminary medical report says he died from an **aortic dissection**. This is a serious condition where the large blood vessel from the heart tears. It can cause sudden and severe health problems. Senator Graham was an important political figure, so his death has shocked many people in the US. Understanding his cause of death helps doctors and the public learn more about this dangerous condition. It also reminds everyone to pay attention to heart health. This news may lead to more awareness and research about aortic dissections in the future.

## 3. U.S. Job Market Shows Signs of Slowing Hiring in June  
  

In June, U.S. employers started to **pull back on hiring**, which means fewer new jobs were offered. This is a change compared to previous months when hiring was stronger. The number of job openings rose only slightly, but new hiring slowed down. The White House says the labor market is still on an **upward trajectory**, meaning things might improve soon. This trend is important because jobs affect many people’s lives and the economy. Experts will watch the job market closely to see if this slowdown continues or if hiring picks up again.
