## 1. Trump’s Payout Fund Faces Uncertainty Among Republicans  
  

Donald Trump’s fund that helps pay legal fees is now on **shaky ground**. Some Republican leaders want to **push for a retreat** from supporting the fund. This means they want to step back and reduce their involvement. The fund was created to help Trump with lawsuits and legal costs. However, many politicians are worried it might bring problems or bad attention. This situation shows how political support can quickly change. The future of the fund is unclear, and it might lose important supporters soon.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **shaky ground** — 불안정한 상태 또는 기반  
>   _The company is on **shaky ground** because of falling sales._  
> - **push for a retreat** — 철수를 요구하다, 후퇴를 추진하다  
>   _The group decided to **push for a retreat** after facing strong opposition._  
> - **step back** — 한 발 물러나다, 거리를 두다  
>   _Sometimes it’s best to **step back** and think before acting._  
> - **bring problems** — 문제를 일으키다  
>   _The new policy could **bring problems** for small businesses._

## 3. US Job Growth Expected to Rise in May Report  
  

Economists expect the US job market to grow in the next report for May. This means more people will find jobs soon. Job growth is important because it shows the economy is doing well. When more people have jobs, they can spend money, which helps businesses. The report can affect decisions by the government and companies. Still, some challenges remain, like changes in industries and worker skills. Watching the job market helps everyone understand how the country’s economy is moving.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습

> - **job growth** — 일자리 증가

## 4. Indian Engineers Question Moving to Silicon Valley  
  

Many Indian engineers once dreamed of working in Silicon Valley. Now, some say, "I would not come to the U.S." They feel the work culture is very different from what they expected. Long hours and high stress are common. Some believe it is hard to keep a good balance between work and life. The rise of tech jobs in India also offers good opportunities at home. This change shows how the global tech world is moving. Engineers are thinking more about quality of life, not just jobs.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **work culture** — 직장 문화  
>   _The **work culture** in the company is very friendly._  
> - **long hours** — 긴 근무 시간  
>   _She works **long hours** but still enjoys her job._  
> - **work-life balance** — 일과 삶의 균형  
>   _Many people want better **work-life balance**._  
> - **good opportunities** — 좋은 기회  
>   _There are **good opportunities** for engineers in many countries._
