## 2. How New Immigration Rules Could Slow U.S. Population Growth ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-28-2.png)

New rules proposed by the Trump administration could **sharply reduce** the number of people coming legally to the U.S. These restrictions target legal immigration and may limit visas for workers and families. If these rules pass, the U.S. population growth could slow down a lot. This matters because immigration has been a key part of America’s growth and economy. Experts worry this could affect the workforce and communities across the country. The government says they want to control immigration better, but critics say it may hurt the country. We will see how Congress and the courts respond to these changes.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **sharply reduce** — 급격히 줄이다  
>   _New laws could **sharply reduce** the number of new immigrants._
> - **legal immigration** — 합법적 이민  
>   _The country depends on **legal immigration** for growth._
> - **population growth** — 인구 증가  
>   _Immigration affects **population growth** in many ways._
> - **workforce** — 노동력

## 3. Most US Job Seekers Want Formal AI Training ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-28-3.png)

A new study shows that 83% of job seekers in the U.S. want **formal AI training**. This means they want official classes or programs to learn about artificial intelligence. AI is becoming important in many jobs, so workers want to be ready for the future. Training can help people get better jobs or keep their current jobs safe from automation. Companies may also benefit by having employees with AI skills. This shows how the job market is changing fast because of technology. It is likely that more schools and companies will offer AI training soon.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **formal AI training** — 공식적인 AI 교육  
>   _Many workers ask for **formal AI training** to improve their skills._
> - **job seekers** — 구직자  
>   _**Job seekers** are looking for new ways to stand out._
> - **automation** — 자동화  
>   _**Automation** is changing how many jobs work._
> - **job market** — 취업 시장

## 4. The Rise of the “Silicon Valley Right” and Its Impact on Tech ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-28-4.png)

A new political group called the “Silicon Valley Right” is **reshaping** the tech world. This group has different political ideas than the traditional views in Silicon Valley. They want more conservative policies in tech and business. This change could affect how tech companies work and what they support. It is important because Silicon Valley plays a big role in the U.S. economy and innovation. Some people welcome this shift, while others worry it may cause conflicts. The tech industry may look very different in the near future because of this.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **reshaping** — 재형성하다, 변화시키다  
>   _New ideas are **reshaping** the future of technology._
> - **conservative policies** — 보수적인 정책  
>   _The group supports **conservative policies** in business._
> - **innovation** — 혁신  
>   _**Innovation** is key to Silicon Valley’s success._
> - **play a big role** — 중요한 역할을 하다
