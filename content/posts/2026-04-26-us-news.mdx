## 1. White House Dinner Interrupted by Shots Fired ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-26-1.png)

Last night, a frightening event happened at the White House Correspondents’ Dinner. Shots were fired nearby, causing panic. Former President Trump was **rushed off stage** quickly to keep him safe. The police responded fast to find the shooter, but thankfully, no one else was hurt. This event shocked many because the dinner is usually a peaceful gathering of journalists and politicians. It shows how security is very important at public events. Officials are now checking how this happened and how to prevent it in the future. People are watching closely to see what new safety rules might be made.

## 2. New Immigration Limits Could Slow U.S. Population Growth ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-26-2.png)

The U.S. government is planning new rules to **restrict legal immigration**. These changes could make it harder for people to move to the U.S. legally. Experts say this might cause a big **reduction in population growth** over the next years. Immigration has been one of the main reasons the U.S. population keeps growing. If fewer people come, it could affect the economy and workforce. Some say this will protect American jobs, but others worry it will hurt businesses that need workers. The government is still discussing these plans, and many people are watching to see what happens next.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **restrict legal immigration** — 합법적 이민을 제한하다  
>   _The government wants to **restrict legal immigration** to control population._  
> - **reduction in population growth** — 인구 증가 감소  
>   _New laws could cause a **reduction in population growth**._  
> - **affect the economy** — 경제에 영향을 미치다  
>   _Less immigration could **affect the economy** negatively._  
> - **watching to see** — 지켜보고 있다  
>   _People are **watching to see** how the new rules will work._

## 3. Nevada Leads the Nation in Job Growth for 2025 ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-26-3.png)

Nevada is the fastest-growing state for jobs in 2025. It **leads the US in job growth** with many new positions opening. This growth is a sign of a strong local economy and businesses expanding. ## 4. Tech Workers Once Sought-After, Now Face Unemployment Challenges ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-26-4.png)

Tech workers in America are facing a tough time. Once, they were the most **sought-after workers** in the country. But now many of these skilled workers are **unemployable** or find it hard to get jobs. Companies have changed how they hire, and some big tech firms have slowed down hiring or laid off staff. This situation worries workers and experts because the tech industry is very important to the economy. Some say new skills or training will be needed to get back into the market. The story shows how fast changes in technology and business can affect people’s jobs.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **sought-after workers** — 인기 있는 노동자들  
>   _Engineers are very **sought-after workers** in many industries._  
> - **unemployable** — 취업할 수 없는  
>   _After the company closed, many workers became **unemployable**._  
> - **laid off staff** — 직원을 해고하다  
>   _The company **laid off staff** due to budget cuts._  
> - **get back into the market** — 다시 시장에 진입하다  
>   _Workers need new skills to **get back into the market**._
