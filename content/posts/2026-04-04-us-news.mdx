## 1. White House Proposes Big Increase in Military Spending  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-04-1.png)

The White House wants to raise the US military budget by **40 percent**. This is a large increase compared to previous years. Officials say the extra money will help the military prepare for new threats and improve equipment. The move comes during a time of global uncertainty, with tensions rising in several regions. Some people support the plan because they want the country to be stronger. Others worry that spending so much money may affect other important areas like education and health. The government still needs approval from Congress before the increase becomes final. If approved, it will likely change the country’s defense strategy for years to come.

## 2. Immigration Policies Affecting Education Access for Children  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-04-2.png)

New federal and state policies are making it harder for immigrant children to get a good education. These rules limit access to schools and certain programs. Experts say this change **erodes decades of progress** in helping children learn and grow. Some schools face pressure to report students who are immigrants. Families feel worried and uncertain about their future in the US. This issue has caused public debate about fairness and children’s rights. Many believe that education should be open to all children, no matter where they come from. The situation may affect not only immigrant families but the whole community’s future.

## 3. US Job Market Shows Strong Growth Despite Concerns  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-04-3.png)

The US job market is doing well, with many new jobs added recently. Reports say the economy created thousands of jobs in March, beating expectations. This shows that businesses are still hiring even with global problems like the war in Iran. However, some experts warn that the future is uncertain because of these risks. The unemployment rate remains low, which is a good sign for workers. Still, people are watching closely to see if this positive trend continues in the coming months. The strong job market gives hope but also calls for caution.

## 4. Tech Industry Sees More Job Cuts as AI Changes Work  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-04-4.png)

Tech companies are cutting jobs more often as they adopt artificial intelligence (AI). Many workers in Silicon Valley and other tech hubs have lost their jobs recently. This is because AI is changing how companies work and what skills they need. Some businesses say they need fewer employees because machines can do some tasks faster. Others support AI for helping create new products and services. The future of work in tech looks different now, and many people are learning new skills to stay competitive. The changes may cause challenges but also new opportunities.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **job cuts** — 일자리 감축  
>   _The company announced **job cuts** to reduce costs._  
> - **adopt artificial intelligence** — 인공지능을 도입하다  
>   _Many businesses **adopt artificial intelligence** to improve efficiency._  
> - **stay competitive** — 경쟁력을 유지하다  
>   _Workers need to learn new skills to **stay competitive**._  
> - **create new opportunities** — 새로운 기회를 만들다  
>   _Technology can **create new opportunities** for young people._
