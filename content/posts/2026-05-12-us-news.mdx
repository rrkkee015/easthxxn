## 1. Iran Conflict Continues as Trump Calls Ceasefire “on Life Support”  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-05-12-1.png)  

Tensions between the US and Iran remain very high. Iran promises to **fight on** despite calls for peace. Former President Trump said the ceasefire talks are barely alive and called them **“on life support.”** This means the peace agreement is very weak and may fail soon. The conflict affects global oil prices and world safety. Many worry the fighting could get worse. People hope leaders find a way to stop the violence. The situation is still very uncertain and could change quickly.

## 2. Court Rules Trump’s Immigration Detention Policy Unconstitutional  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-05-12-2.png)  

A US court has said that Trump’s policy for **mandatory detention** of some immigrants is not legal. This policy required certain immigrants to stay in jail while waiting for their cases. The court said this rule goes against the law and is **unconstitutional**. This decision is important because it may change how the government treats immigrants in the future. It could lead to fewer people being kept in detention. Immigration groups welcomed the ruling as a step for better rights. The government may appeal or change the policy soon.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **mandatory detention** — 의무 구금  
>   _The law requires **mandatory detention** for certain offenders._  
> - **unconstitutional** — 위헌의, 헌법에 어긋나는  
>   _The court found the new law **unconstitutional**._  
> - **appeal** — 항소하다, 재심을 요청하다  
>   _The company decided to **appeal** the court’s decision._

## 3. Strong US Job Growth Surprises Experts in April  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-05-12-3.png)  

The US job market is doing better than many people expected. In April, employers added 115,000 new jobs, which is a **surprisingly strong** number. The unemployment rate stayed low. This shows the economy is still growing and companies are hiring. The good job numbers may encourage people to spend more money. It also means workers may feel more confident about their future. Experts will watch if this trend continues in the coming months.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 일자리 시장, 고용시장  
>   _The **job market** is slowly improving this year._  
> - **surprisingly strong** — 놀라울 만큼 강한  
>   _The team showed a **surprisingly strong** performance._  
> - **unemployment rate** — 실업률

## 4. “Silicon Valley Right” Changes the US Tech Scene  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-05-12-4.png)  

A new political group called the “Silicon Valley Right” is starting to change the technology world in the US. This group has different ideas than the usual tech leaders. They want to take a stronger political position, especially about China. This shift affects how companies work and how the US competes with other countries. It also changes the culture inside the tech industry. Many experts say this new group will **reshape** the tech landscape in important ways. We can expect more political debates in tech soon.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **reshape** — 재형성하다, 바꾸다  
>   _The new policy will **reshape** the education system._  
> - **tech landscape** — 기술 분야의 전반적인 상황  
>   _The **tech landscape** has changed a lot in the last five years._  
> - **political position** — 정치적 입장  
>   _The company took a clear **political position** on climate change._
