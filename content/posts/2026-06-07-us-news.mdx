## 1. Shooting Incident Near Festival in Toledo Raises Safety Concerns  
  

Several people were shot near a popular street festival in Toledo, Ohio. The police said the shooting happened late in the evening and caused panic among festival visitors. Authorities are still investigating what caused the violence and are working to find the suspects. This incident has made many people worry about safety at public events. Officials say they will increase security to prevent similar problems in the future. The festival was a chance for the community to come together, but now it has a shadow of fear. The police are asking anyone who saw something to come forward to help solve the case.

## 2. Court Orders Restart of U.S. Asylum and Immigration Processing  
  

A judge has ruled that the U.S. government must restart asylum and immigration processing. This means people seeking protection or legal entry can begin their applications again. The government had paused many immigration services recently, causing delays and uncertainty. The judge’s decision is important because it gives hope to many immigrants who had been waiting for answers. Immigration is a big topic in the U.S., and policies often change. This court order may lead to faster processing and better support for immigrants. Officials now have to follow this new rule and adjust their work.

## 3. U.S. Adds 172,000 Jobs, Mostly in Restaurants, Bars, and Hotels  
  

The U.S. economy added 172,000 new jobs last month. Many of these jobs were in restaurants, bars, and hotels. This shows that the service industry is growing again after some slow months. Job growth is important because it helps people earn money and supports the economy. However, experts say the job market is still not as strong as before the pandemic. Some workers are still worried about job security and wages. The government and businesses will watch these numbers closely to plan for the future.

## 4. Tech Industry Scores Big Win in California After Primary Election  
  

The tech industry won important support in California’s recent primary election. Many tech-friendly candidates won their races after millions of dollars were spent on campaigns. This victory is good news for tech companies because these leaders often support policies that help innovation and growth. California is a major center for technology in the U.S., so these election results could affect many businesses and workers. Some people worry about how this might impact housing and local communities. The tech industry will likely continue to influence politics in the state for years to come.
