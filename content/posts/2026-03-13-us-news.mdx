Now, the woman is suing the government because she believes this treatment is unfair. Many families like hers feel that being separated does not make the country safer. This case highlights ongoing problems in the U.S. immigration system and raises questions about how families are treated. People hope the court case will bring changes to protect immigrant families.

## 3. Job Market Changes Affect the U.S. Economy ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-13-3.png)

The U.S. economy recently lost 92,000 jobs in February. This caused the unemployment rate to rise to 4.4%. Experts say these numbers show challenges for the job market and the economy. Some believe the economy is slowing down and people are worried about finding work. Others say these changes are normal and part of a bigger economic cycle. The job market is important because it affects how much money people earn and how businesses grow. The government and companies will watch these trends closely to decide what to do next.

## 4. U.S. Universities Open Joint Campus in Silicon Valley’s AI Hub ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-13-4.png)

Several U.S. universities have started a new joint campus in Silicon Valley. This campus will focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI) education and research. Silicon Valley is known for technology and innovation, so this is a natural place for such a project. The goal is to train students in AI skills that are very important today. This new campus will help prepare future workers for jobs in tech industries. It may also help the U.S. stay competitive in the global technology race. Many experts believe investing in AI education is key for the country’s future.
