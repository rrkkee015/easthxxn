## 1. US Jobs Market Beats Expectations for Second Month ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-05-10-1.png)

The US jobs market showed strong growth again in April. Employers added about 115,000 new jobs, which surprised many experts who expected fewer. This is the second month in a row that the job numbers have been better than expected. Unemployment stayed low, which means many people still have work. This strong job growth can help the economy because more people earning money can buy more goods and services. However, some worry this might also cause prices to rise faster. Overall, the good job numbers give hope for continued economic recovery after the challenges of recent years.

## 2. Court Blocks Trump Immigration Policy on Bond Hearings ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-05-10-2.png)

A federal appeals court recently rejected a Trump-era immigration policy. This policy had denied bond hearings to many immigrants, meaning they could not ask to be released from detention while waiting for their cases. The court said this policy was unfair and not legal. This decision is important because it affects how immigrants are treated in the United States. It may give more rights to immigrants and change how immigration cases are handled in the future. Immigration is a big topic in the US, and court decisions like this show how the system can change over time.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **bond hearing** — 보석 심리  
>   _The immigrant requested a **bond hearing** to ask for release._  
> - **denying bond hearings** — 보석 심리를 거부하다  
>   _The government was **denying bond hearings** to many detainees._  
> - **rejected a policy** — 정책을 거부하다, 무효로 하다  
>   _The court **rejected the policy** because it was unfair._  
> - **affects how** — ~에 영향을 미치다  
>   _This decision **affects how** immigration cases are processed._

## 3. Putin Suggests Ukraine War Is "Coming to an End" ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-05-10-3.png)

Russian President Vladimir Putin said that the war in Ukraine is **coming to an end**. This is a surprising comment because the war has lasted a long time and caused many problems. Putin did not give many details but hinted that the fighting might slow down soon. The war has affected many countries and caused suffering for many people. If the war does end soon, it could bring peace and help rebuild the region. However, experts are cautious and say it is too early to be sure what will happen next.

## 4. Silicon Valley Leads the Race in AI Technology ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-05-10-4.png)

Silicon Valley in California is known as a major center for technology and innovation. Recently, American companies there have been focusing on artificial intelligence (AI). Some say the US is in a “race” with China to develop the best AI technology. Companies compete to create faster and smarter AI tools. This competition is important because AI will change many parts of life and business in the future. It also brings political and economic challenges as countries try to lead in this powerful technology.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **tech hub** — 기술 중심지  
>   _Silicon Valley is a famous **tech hub**._  
> - **push the idea of** — ~라는 생각을 밀다, 주장하다  
>   _Companies **push the idea of** innovation to attract investors._  
> - **race with** — ~와 경쟁하다  
>   _There is a **race with** other countries to develop new technology._  
> - **lead in technology** — 기술을 선도하다  
>   _The company wants to **lead in technology** globally._
