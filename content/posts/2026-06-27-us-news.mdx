## 1. US Responds with Strikes After Cargo Ship Attack 

The United States carried out military strikes against Iran after an attack damaged an American cargo ship. The attack happened in a busy shipping area, which made many people worried about safety. The US government said the strikes were a way to **address** the threat and protect their interests. This action shows how serious the situation is between the two countries. Experts say this could change how ships move through the area and might cause more conflict. The US wants to stop future attacks and keep trade routes open. People are watching closely to see what Iran will do next.

## 3. Jobless Aid Applications Drop as Layoffs Stay Low

Even though the economy faces some problems, layoffs remain low. Experts say this means the job market is still strong in many areas. However, some workers still find it hard to get good jobs. Employers are being careful about hiring, but they are not cutting many jobs. The situation may change if the economy gets worse or better soon.
