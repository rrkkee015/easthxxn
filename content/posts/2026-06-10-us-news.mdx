## 1. U.S. Launches Strikes on Iran After Helicopter Downed 

The United States recently launched military strikes on Iran. This action came after an American Apache helicopter was downed in a conflict zone. The strikes are a response to what the U.S. calls hostile behavior from Iran. This event increases tensions between the two countries. Experts worry this could lead to more conflict in the Middle East. The U.S. government says the strikes are meant to protect American forces and allies. People around the world are watching closely to see what happens next. This situation shows how quickly international problems can **escalate** into serious actions.

## 2. U.S. Immigration Policies Create Challenges for Visitors 

New U.S. immigration policies are making it hard for some visitors to come to the country. This has been especially clear during the recent World Cup event. Many fans and workers find it difficult to get visas or enter the U.S. The policies are meant to control immigration but also cause a **chilling effect** on tourism and business. Some lawmakers are debating how these rules affect Americans and visitors alike. They want to find a balance between security and welcoming others. The future may bring changes to these policies if pressure grows.

## 3. U.S. Job Market Shows Steady Growth in May 

The U.S. economy added 172,000 jobs in May, showing steady but slower growth. This is good news because it means companies are still hiring, even though inflation is making things harder for consumers. Many workers are finding new opportunities in different industries. Economists say this trend shows the **job market** is strong but cautious. A strong job market helps people earn money and spend it, which supports the economy. However, inflation means prices are higher, so people must be careful with money. Experts will continue to watch job reports to see if this growth continues.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 취업 시장  
>   _The **job market** is improving with more positions available._  
> - **steady growth** — 꾸준한 성장  
>   _The company experienced **steady growth** over the past year._  
> - **slower growth** — 느린 성장

> - **inflation** — 인플레이션, 물가 상승

> - **find new opportunities** — 새로운 기회를 찾다

## 4. Tech Industry Scores Big Wins in California Primaries 

In the latest California primary elections, the tech industry won important victories. Many tech companies spent millions of dollars to support candidates who understand their interests. This shows how much influence the tech world has in politics today. The election results could affect future laws about technology, privacy, and business. Tech leaders want to protect innovation and keep California a global tech hub. Some voters worry that too much money from tech could influence politics too much. This story shows the close connection between technology and government.
