## 3. Why Are So Many Americans Struggling to Find Jobs?  


Even though the U.S. economy is adding jobs, many people find it hard to get work. Employers say they have open positions but still cannot fill them. Some workers may not have the right skills or live far from jobs. Others want better pay or conditions before they accept a position. This creates a problem where jobs exist, but people cannot or will not take them. Experts watch this situation closely because it affects the whole economy. The government may try to help by training workers or improving job offers.

## 4. Europe Changes Its Approach to Big U.S. Tech Companies  


Europe is starting to change how it deals with big U.S. technology companies. Some rules and laws are different now. Europe no longer follows Silicon Valley’s way completely. Instead, it tries to create its own rules to control tech companies better. This is important because these companies have a lot of power and influence worldwide. The change may affect how tech companies work and how people use technology. Many experts watch these developments with interest. It shows how global technology rules are evolving.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **deal with** — 다루다, 처리하다  (TWO trailing spaces here!)  
>   _Governments must **deal with** large technology companies._  
> - **follow the rules** — 규칙을 따르다  (TWO trailing spaces here!)  
>   _Companies should **follow the rules** to protect users._  
> - **create rules** — 규칙을 만들다  (TWO trailing spaces here!)  
>   _Europe wants to **create rules** for tech companies._  
> - **have power** — 권력을 가지다  (TWO trailing spaces here!)  
>   _Big tech firms **have power** over many users._  
> - **evolve** — 진화하다, 발전하다  (TWO trailing spaces here!)  
>   _Technology laws **evolve** as new problems appear._
