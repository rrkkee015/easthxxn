## 1. New Iran Peace Talks Begin in Pakistan ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-25-1.png)

The United States is helping start new peace talks about Iran, with important leaders going to Pakistan to talk. This is a chance for countries to discuss problems and find ways to avoid conflict. The talks involve people from the U.S. and Iran, and many hope this will help calm tensions in the region. These discussions are important because they could affect peace and security in the Middle East. If the talks go well, it might lead to better relationships and fewer problems between the countries. However, the process could take time, and success is not guaranteed yet.

## 2. Nevada Leads the US in Job Growth for 2025 ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-25-2.png)

Nevada is the top state in the United States for job growth in 2025. More companies are hiring workers there, and the number of new jobs is increasing faster than in other states. This is good news for people looking for work in Nevada and shows the state's economy is growing. Experts say this growth comes from several industries, including technology and tourism. However, the job market in the rest of the country is not as strong. This means Nevada might attract more workers from other places soon.

## 3. Court Blocks Trump’s Order Suspending Asylum Access ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-25-3.png)

A court has stopped an executive order from former President Trump that tried to suspend asylum access in the U.S. This means people can still ask for asylum, which is protection for those escaping danger in their home countries. The court said that the order violated immigration laws. This decision is important because it protects the rights of migrants and asylum seekers. It also shows how courts can check the power of the president. The government may try to appeal this decision, so the situation could change in the future.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **executive order** — 행정명령  
>   _The president signed an **executive order** yesterday._  
> - **suspend asylum access** — 망명 접근을 중단하다  
>   _The order would **suspend asylum access** temporarily._  
> - **violated immigration laws** — 이민법을 위반했다  
>   _The court ruled it **violated immigration laws**._  
> - **protect the rights** — 권리를 보호하다  
>   _Laws should **protect the rights** of all people._  
> - **check the power** — 권한을 견제하다  
>   _Courts **check the power** of government leaders._

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces Criticism Over Tech Worker Policies ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-25-4.png)

Silicon Valley, the famous technology center in California, is facing criticism about its hiring of Indian and Chinese tech workers. Former President Trump said that these workers have **destroyed our nation**, blaming them for problems in the tech industry. This has caused a lot of discussion about immigration and work policies in tech companies. Many people think Silicon Valley needs to change how it hires workers and support American workers more. The debate shows the challenge of balancing global talent and local jobs in technology.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **facing criticism** — 비판을 받고 있다  
>   _The company is **facing criticism** for its hiring policies._  
> - **tech worker** — 기술자, IT 노동자  
>   _Many **tech workers** come from different countries._  
> - **blaming them for** — ~에 대해 그들을 비난하다  
>   _He is **blaming them for** the industry’s problems._  
> - **support American workers** — 미국 노동자를 지원하다  
>   _Policies should **support American workers** fairly._  
> - **balancing global talent** — 세계 인재의 균형을 맞추다  
>   _It is hard **balancing global talent** and local jobs._
