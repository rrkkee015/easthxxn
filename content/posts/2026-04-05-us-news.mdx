## 1. Tensions Rise as US Searches for Missing Airman Near Iran ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-05-1.png)

The United States is under pressure as it searches for a missing airman after a second fighter jet was downed near the Strait of Hormuz. This area is very important because it is a key waterway for world oil trade. The US and Israel are trying to put **pressure** on Iran to find the airman before a deadline. The situation is dangerous because tensions between the countries are already high. The US government is working hard to get answers and hopes to avoid further conflict. This story matters because it could affect peace in the region and global oil prices. People will watch closely what happens next in these talks and searches.

## 2. Foreign Doctors Face Challenges Due to Trump’s Immigration Policy ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-05-2.png)

Many foreign doctors have been forced to leave American hospitals because of changes in immigration rules during the Trump administration. These doctors often help with important medical care, especially where there are not enough American doctors. The policy made it harder for them to get work visas and stay in the US. Hospitals now face staff shortages, which can affect patient care. This problem shows how immigration laws can affect everyday life and health services. Some experts say the policy needs to change to support hospitals better. This story is important because it affects both doctors’ careers and public health.

## 3. Job Market Shows Strong Growth in Latest Report ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-05-3.png)

This growth means the economy is doing well and more people are finding work. The report also showed that some industries are hiring more than others. This is good news for workers looking for jobs and for the country’s overall economic health. However, experts still watch for signs of future changes because the job market can change quickly. It is important to keep an eye on these trends.

## 4. Silicon Valley’s Political Shift: Power and Influence in Tech ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-05-4.png)

Silicon Valley, known for its technology companies, is showing signs of a political change. Some say it is shifting to the right, while others believe the change is about gaining more **power**. Many tech workers and companies are involved in politics behind the scenes. They support groups and ideas that may help their business and influence government decisions. This shift is interesting because Silicon Valley used to be seen as mostly liberal. Understanding this change helps us see how technology and politics connect. It may affect future laws and the tech industry’s role in the US.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **shifting to the right** — (정치적으로) 보수 쪽으로 움직이다  
>   _Some voters are **shifting to the right** this election season._  
> - **power** — 힘, 권력  
>   _The company wants more **power** in the market._  
> - **behind the scenes** — 비공개로, 막후에서  
>   _Decisions are often made **behind the scenes**._  
> - **gain influence** — 영향력을 얻다  
>   _The group works to **gain influence** in government._
