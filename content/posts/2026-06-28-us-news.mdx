## 1. Supreme Court Supports Trump’s Immigration Policy Expansion  
  

The U.S. Supreme Court recently made a decision that helps the Trump administration restart a strict immigration policy. This policy limits some people from entering the United States. The court’s decision means the government has more power to control immigration rules. Some experts warn this could lead to fewer people moving to the U.S., which might affect the population size over time. Immigration has been a big topic in American politics for years. This change shows how the courts can influence important policies. We may see more legal battles and debates about immigration in the near future.

People are interested in this area because it creates new kinds of jobs and technology. This growth could bring more opportunities for workers in the future. It also shows how the job market can be different from the stock market.

## 4. Trump-Backed Julia Letlow Wins Louisiana Senate Runoff  
  

Julia Letlow, a candidate supported by former President Trump, has won the runoff election for a U.S. Senate seat in Louisiana. A runoff happens when no one wins more than half the votes in the first election, so the top two candidates compete again. Letlow’s win means she will likely become a senator. This election is important because it shows how Trump’s influence remains strong in some areas. Many people watched the race closely as it could change political power in the Senate. Letlow promised to continue working on issues important to her voters.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **runoff election** — 결선 투표  
>   _The candidates will compete again in the **runoff election**._  
> - **candidate** — 후보자  
>   _She is a strong **candidate** for the Senate._  
> - **political power** — 정치적 권력  
>   _Winning the seat gives her more **political power**._  
> - **influence** — 영향력  
>   _Trump’s **influence** helped her win the election._
