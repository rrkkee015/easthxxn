## 1. Colorado Governor Reduces Sentence for Voting Machine Case  

The Colorado governor decided to **commute** the prison sentence of Tina Peters. She was found guilty of **tampering** with voting machines, which means she tried to change or damage the machines used in elections. This case is important because it involves trust in the voting system. Many people are worried about election security and fairness. The governor’s decision means Tina Peters will spend less time in prison. Some people support this move, while others think it is wrong. This story shows how political decisions affect the justice system and public opinion. We will see how this affects future cases about election safety.

## 4. Indian Tech Workers in the U.S. Fear Toxic Work Culture  

Many Indian tech workers in the U.S. worry about returning to work in India because of a **toxic work culture** there. They say the culture in India is very different, with more focus on strict rules and hierarchy. In the U.S., tech companies often have a more **no-nonsense** and flexible style. This change makes workers feel stressed if they move back. The story shows how work culture affects people’s happiness and job choices. Companies may need to improve their cultures to keep good workers happy.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **toxic work culture** — 독한 직장 문화  
>   _Many workers want to avoid a **toxic work culture**._  
> - **no-nonsense** — 진지하고 실용적인
