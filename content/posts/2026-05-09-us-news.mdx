## 1. Virginia Court Changes US House Voting Maps ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-05-09-1.png)

The Virginia Supreme Court made a big decision by striking down the new voting maps drawn by Democrats. These maps decide how the US House of Representatives districts are divided in Virginia. The court said the Democratic maps were not fair. This gives a win to the Republicans because the old maps will be used instead. This is important because voting maps can affect which party wins elections. The change could influence future elections in Virginia and possibly other states. People are watching closely to see how this affects political power in the US.

## 3. Federal Appeals Court Blocks Trump Immigration Policy ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-05-09-3.png)

A federal appeals court recently rejected a Trump-era immigration policy. This policy denied bond hearings for some immigrants, making it harder for them to be released from detention. The court said this policy was not legal and stopped it. Bond hearings are important because they let immigrants ask to be free while waiting for court decisions. This ruling could impact many immigrants and how immigration cases are handled in the future. The government may need to change its rules to follow the court’s decision.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **reject** — 거부하다, 기각하다  
>   _The judge decided to **reject** the unfair policy._  
> - **bond hearing** — 보석 심리  
>   _A **bond hearing** allows the immigrant to ask for release._  
> - **detention** — 구금, 감금  
>   _Many immigrants face long **detention** periods._  
> - **ruling** — 판결, 결정  
>   _The court’s **ruling** changed the immigration rules._  
> - **policy** — 정책  
>   _The government created a new **policy** on visas._

## 4. US Job Market Shows Strong Growth Again ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-05-09-4.png)

The US job market is doing better than many experts expected for the second month in a row. Recent reports show that companies hired more workers than predicted. This is a positive sign for the economy because more jobs mean people can earn money and spend more. However, some challenges still remain, like higher prices and worker shortages in some areas. Economists will watch the job market closely to see if this growth continues. If it does, it could help the US economy stay strong in the coming months.

> - **job market** — 취업 시장
