## 1. U.S. Lets Russian Oil Tanker Reach Cuba Despite Blockade  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-30-1.png)

The U.S. recently allowed a Russian oil tanker to arrive in Cuba. This is notable because the U.S. has a blockade on Cuba and is trying to limit oil shipments to the country. The decision shows the complicated balance between politics and economics. Some experts think this move could affect U.S. relations with other countries. The U.S. wants to keep pressure on Russia but also avoid making the situation worse in Cuba. It is unclear if more shipments will be allowed in the future. This story is important because it shows how global events affect American policies. People will watch closely to see what happens next.

## 2. Women Now Outnumber Men in the U.S. Workforce  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-03-30-2.png)

For the first time, there are more women than men working in the United States. This change is happening because more women are joining the workforce and some men are leaving jobs or retiring earlier. Many women work in healthcare, education, and service jobs. This shift affects how companies hire and manage workers. Experts say it could change the economy and family life in many ways. Women’s growing role in the workforce shows progress but also challenges, like equal pay. People are curious to see how this will shape the future of work.

## 3. What You Need to Know About the Supreme Court’s Asylum Case  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-30-3.png)

The U.S. Supreme Court is hearing an important case about asylum seekers. The case is about whether the government can turn back people at the border who want to apply for asylum. Asylum seekers are people who leave their country because of danger or fear. This case could change how the U.S. handles immigration and border security. Many groups are watching because the decision will affect thousands of people. The Court's ruling might make it easier or harder to get asylum. This is a key issue in American immigration law today.

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces Tough Job Market Amid AI Competition  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-30-4.png)

Silicon Valley is known for tech jobs, but right now many people find it hard to get hired there. Hundreds of people apply for each job, and competition is very strong. New technology like artificial intelligence (AI) is changing the work landscape. Some companies are hiring fewer workers while trying to develop better AI tools. This creates uncertainty for tech workers. The situation shows how fast technology can change the job market. Many workers are learning new skills to stay competitive. This story shows the challenges in the tech industry today.
