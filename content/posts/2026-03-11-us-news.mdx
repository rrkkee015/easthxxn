## 1. Senate Republicans Target Immigration Enforcement Policies ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-11-1.png)

The U.S. Senate Republicans have introduced new efforts to challenge state and local rules about immigration enforcement. These policies affect how local police work with federal immigration authorities. Some cities called "sanctuary cities" limit their cooperation with immigration officials. Republicans argue these policies can make it harder to keep communities safe. They want stronger laws to support immigration enforcement at all levels. This issue is important because it affects many immigrants and how laws are applied across the country. The debate will likely continue as politicians discuss the balance between safety and immigrant rights.

## 2. The U.S. Job Market Faces Unexpected Job Losses ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-03-11-2.png)

Recently, the United States lost about 92,000 jobs, which was a surprise to many experts. Usually, the job market grows, but this drop is a warning sign about the economy's health. The unemployment rate also rose to 4.4%, meaning more people are looking for work but cannot find jobs. This news makes many people worry about a possible economic slowdown or recession. Experts say several factors, like changes in industries or global issues, might be causing these problems. The government and businesses will watch the job market closely to decide what actions to take next.

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces Risks from Taiwan Chip Shortage ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-11-4.png)

Silicon Valley, known as a center for technology and innovation, is facing a possible crisis. Much of the world’s computer chips come from factories in Taiwan. If these factories have problems, it can cause a shortage of chips worldwide. This shortage could slow down the growth of tech companies in Silicon Valley. Experts warn that this risk has been ignored for too long. The chip shortage shows how global events can affect local industries. Companies are now thinking about how to make their supply chains stronger to avoid future problems.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
