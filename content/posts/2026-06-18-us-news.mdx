## 1. Trump Signs Official US-Iran Agreement Paperwork 

The former President Donald Trump recently signed a **hard copy** of the US-Iran agreement. This document is an official paper version of a deal between the two countries. The agreement aims to reduce tensions and set rules for cooperation. It is important because it may help prevent conflict in the Middle East. The deal also affects global politics and the economy, as Iran is a major oil producer. The signing shows both sides want to follow the agreement strictly. However, there might still be challenges ahead as trust between the countries is low.

## 2. A Quick Look at US Immigration History and Its Challenges 

The history of US immigration policy shows many changes over time. The article explains how rules started and why some people feel worried about immigrants now. It talks about the roots of “nativism,” which means favoring people born in the country over newcomers. These ideas became stronger during Trump’s presidency. Immigration laws can be strict and create **barriers** for people who want to live in the US. The article helps us understand why immigration is a big issue in politics. Learning about history can help us think about better solutions for the future.

## 3. The US Job Market Shows Strong Growth Despite Challenges 

The US job market is recovering well after difficult times. In May, 172,000 new jobs were added, showing that the economy is **resilient**. Even though there are worries about conflicts like the Iran war and rising prices, many Americans still find work. However, some people feel **frustrated** because the cost of living is high. The steady job growth is a good sign for the country’s future. Experts say the economy is improving but also need to watch inflation and wages closely. This news gives hope to job seekers and businesses.

## 4. US Limits Foreign Access to Advanced AI Models 

The US government has placed new **restrictions** on foreign companies trying to use advanced AI models from a company called Anthropic. These AI tools are very powerful and important for technology development. The goal is to protect American technology and prevent it from being used in ways that could harm US interests. This move has caused some concern in Silicon Valley, where many tech companies work with international partners. The situation shows how technology and politics are closely linked today. It may affect how AI business grows in the future.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **restrictions** — 제한, 규제  
>   _There are new **restrictions** on travel during the pandemic._  
> - **foreign companies** — 외국 회사들
