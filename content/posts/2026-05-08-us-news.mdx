## 1. US-Iran Ceasefire Holds Despite Tensions in Strait of Hormuz  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-05-08-1.png)  

The US and Iran recently exchanged fire near the Strait of Hormuz, a very important waterway for global oil trade. Despite this, former President Trump said the **ceasefire** between the two countries is still in place. This means both sides have agreed to stop fighting, even though there are some **tensions**. The area is often a hotspot for conflicts, so the news is important for global peace. Experts are watching closely because any new conflict could affect oil prices worldwide. The US wants to keep the situation calm to avoid a bigger crisis. This story shows how delicate international relations can be and why peace agreements matter.

## 2. Court Rules Trump’s 10% Global Tariff Illegal  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-05-08-2.png)  

A US trade court recently said that the 10% tariff imposed by Trump on many global imports is **illegal**. The tariff was meant to protect American businesses by making foreign goods more expensive. However, the court found that this tariff broke trade rules. This ruling is important because it could affect many companies and trade agreements. The government might have to remove or change the tariff soon. Trade experts say this decision could change how the US handles tariffs in the future. It also shows how laws can limit government power in trade matters.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습

## 3. US Job Market Shows Mixed Signs in April  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-05-08-3.png)  

The job market in the US is showing some **unusual** patterns this April. Hiring has increased, meaning more people are getting jobs, but at the same time, the market feels **stuck**. Many companies still have open positions but are not hiring as fast as expected. Some reports say there are fewer job cuts compared to before, which is a good sign. However, experts call the situation **uncomfortable** because it is not clear if the economy will get stronger soon. This story is important because jobs affect many people's lives and the whole economy. Watching the job market helps us understand how the country is doing.

## 4. Tech Events Moving Away from Silicon Valley  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-05-08-4.png)  

Many big tech companies are now **shifting** their events and conferences outside of Silicon Valley. Silicon Valley has been the center of the tech world for many years. But recently, companies want to reach more people and reduce costs. Moving events helps them connect with different communities across the US. It also shows how the tech industry is **reshaping** itself after years of growth. Experts believe this trend will continue as technology spreads to new regions. This change may create new opportunities for smaller cities and startups.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **shifting** — 옮기다, 이동하다  (two spaces)
