## 1. Illinois Lt. Governor Wins Democratic Senate Primary ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-18-1.png)

Juliana Stratton, the Lieutenant Governor of Illinois, recently won the Democratic Senate primary. This means she will likely be the Democratic candidate in the upcoming Senate election. Stratton is known for her work on social justice and economic equality. This win is important because it shows the voters' support for her ideas and leadership. The Senate seat is open because the current senator decided not to run again. Stratton will now prepare for the general election against candidates from other parties. Winning the Senate seat could give her a bigger role in national politics. This election could affect laws about healthcare, education, and jobs.

Immigration is an important topic in the United States. Many people want to know the facts and numbers about immigrants living in the country. The Migration Policy Institute shared statistics that help explain who immigrants are and how they contribute to the economy. For example, immigrants work in many industries, start businesses, and pay taxes. The data also shows trends in legal immigration and temporary visas. Understanding these facts helps people discuss immigration policies better. It is important because immigration affects schools, healthcare, and jobs. Knowing the truth can reduce misunderstandings and support fair decisions.

## 3. The Surprising Job Loss in the US Economy ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-18-3.png)

Last month, the United States lost 92,000 jobs unexpectedly. This was a surprise because economists predicted job growth instead. Losing jobs is a concern because it can slow down the economy. When people lose jobs, they spend less money, which affects many businesses. The unemployment rate also rose to 4.4 percent. Experts say this may be a sign of economic trouble ahead. The government and companies will watch these numbers closely. They may change policies to help create more jobs and support workers.

## 4. Silicon Valley Faces a Growing Chip Crisis ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-18-4.png)

Silicon Valley is famous as the center of technology and innovation in the US. But it faces a serious problem with computer chips. Many chips come from Taiwan, and recent issues there could cause a big shortage. This shortage can slow down the production of computers, phones, and cars. Silicon Valley companies have long ignored this risk. Now, the problem is clear, and they must find new ways to make or get chips. This situation shows how important supply chains are for technology. If the chip crisis continues, it could affect many industries and the economy.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **chip shortage** — 칩 부족  
>   _The **chip shortage** is delaying the production of new phones._  
> - **supply chain** — 공급망  
>   _A broken **supply chain** can stop factories from working._  
> - **innovation** — 혁신  
>   _Silicon Valley is known for its **innovation** in technology._  
> - **production** — 생산  
>   _The company increased **production** after solving the chip problem._
