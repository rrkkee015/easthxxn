## 2. Foreign Doctors Forced Out of U.S. Hospitals by Immigration Policy ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-06-2.png)

Many foreign doctors are leaving U.S. hospitals because of new immigration rules from the Trump era. These doctors had been important in caring for patients, especially during the pandemic. The policy changes made it harder for them to stay or get work visas. Hospitals now face shortages of skilled medical workers. This problem could affect the quality of healthcare in the U.S. Some people want the government to change these rules to bring back foreign doctors. The story shows how immigration policies can directly influence the health system.

## 4. Silicon Valley’s Political Shift: Power and Influence at the Center ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-06-4.png)

Silicon Valley may be changing its political views, moving toward more focus on power and influence. Some say the tech industry is less about left or right politics and more about gaining control. This includes support for new AI companies like Anthropic. Tech workers are also active in city politics, including campaigns about immigration enforcement. These changes show how technology and politics are connected in new ways. The future of Silicon Valley could affect not only tech but also national politics. This shift matters for workers, companies, and the public.

> ### 영어 표현 학습

> - **focus on power and influence** — 권력과 영향력에 집중하다
