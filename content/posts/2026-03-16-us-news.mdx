## 1. US-China Summit May Face Delay Amid Strait of Hormuz Pressure  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-16-1.png)

The upcoming US-China summit might be delayed as the United States puts **pressure** on China to help reopen the Strait of Hormuz. This sea route is very important for world oil trade, and it has been blocked recently. The US hopes China will use its influence to ease tensions and allow ships to pass safely. President Trump suggested the meeting could be postponed until progress happens. This situation is a part of bigger global conflicts involving Iran and other countries. Many people watch this closely because it affects world peace and the global economy. If the summit is delayed, talks between the two countries might slow down, which could cause more uncertainty.

![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-16-3.png)

However, some businesses are not actually hiring more people yet. The rise in job openings shows that the job market is still strong even though the economy faces challenges. More job openings give workers more chances to find work. But companies may be careful about hiring because of economic uncertainty. ## 4. Silicon Valley Faces Risks Over Taiwan Chip Shortage  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-16-4.png)

Silicon Valley, known as America’s tech hub, may face serious problems because of a possible chip shortage from Taiwan. Taiwan makes many important microchips used in smartphones, computers, and cars. If a disaster happens in Taiwan, it could stop chip production for a long time. Silicon Valley companies depend on these chips for their products and growth. Many experts say this risk has been ignored for years. Now, some US universities are working to create new AI campuses in Silicon Valley to support innovation despite these challenges. This situation shows how connected the world is and why tech companies must plan carefully for the future.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **tech hub** — 기술 중심지  
>   _Silicon Valley is a famous **tech hub** in the United States._  
> - **chip shortage** — 칩 부족  
>   _The car industry is affected by a global **chip shortage**._  
> - **innovation** — 혁신, 새로움  
>   _The company is known for its **innovation** in mobile phones._
