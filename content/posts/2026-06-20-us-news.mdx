## 3. Europe Changes Relationship with US Big Tech 

Europe is starting to **break up with** some big American technology companies. This means Europe wants to have more control and not depend only on US tech firms. However, Europe still follows many of the rules and ideas that come from Silicon Valley, the center of US tech innovation. This shows that even when countries want to be independent, it is hard to change how the tech world works. The article explains how regulations and politics shape this relationship. This change could affect how companies work globally and how users get their services. The future will show if Europe can create a different tech path.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **break up with** — (~와) 결별하다, 관계를 끊다  
>   _She decided to **break up with** her boyfriend last week._  
> - **have control** — 통제권을 가지다  
>   _The company wants to **have control** over its data._  
> - **follow rules** — 규칙을 따르다  
>   _All players must **follow rules** during the game._  
> - **shape relationship** — 관계를 형성하다  
>   _Trust can **shape relationships** between people._

## 4. Indian Engineers Question Moving to Silicon Valley 

Many Indian engineers spent years dreaming of working in Silicon Valley, the famous tech hub in the US. But now, some of them say they **would not come to** the US anymore. Reasons include high costs, difficult work visas, and changing job opportunities. This is a big change because Silicon Valley has been a top place for tech workers worldwide. The article explains how the tech landscape is changing and how it affects careers. It also shows how some engineers prefer to work in other countries or remotely. This shift might affect the future of technology and innovation in the US.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **would not come to** — ~에 오지 않을 것이다 (거절 의사 표현)  
>   _He said he **would not come to** the party tonight._  
> - **dream of** — ~을 꿈꾸다  
>   _Many students **dream of** becoming doctors._  
> - **work visa** — 취업 비자  
>   _Getting a **work visa** can be a long process._  
> - **changing job opportunities** — 변화하는 일자리 기회  
>   _The city offers many **changing job opportunities**._
