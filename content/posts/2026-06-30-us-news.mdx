## 1. Supreme Court Expands President’s Control Over Federal Agencies  
  

The U.S. Supreme Court has made a decision that gives the president more **power** over federal government agencies. This means the president can have a stronger say in how these agencies work and make rules. The court’s ruling changes how much control Congress and courts have over the federal bureaucracy. Supporters say it helps the government work better and faster, but critics worry it could give too much **influence** to the president. This case is important because it affects how laws and policies are carried out every day. In the future, this could lead to bigger changes in how federal agencies operate. Many people are watching closely to see what happens next.

## 2. Family Members Speak Out About Sanctuary City Policies  
  

Recently, family members of victims affected by crimes have shared their thoughts about sanctuary city policies. Sanctuary cities limit cooperation with federal immigration authorities. Some believe these policies protect immigrant communities, but others say they can put people at risk. The families testified to lawmakers, asking for changes to these policies because they feel **strongly** that public safety should come first. The debate is very emotional and political. It is important because it affects many people living in the U.S., especially immigrants and local communities. Lawmakers will use these stories to decide what to do next about immigration laws.

> - **sanctuary policies** — 보호 정책 (TWO spaces)

## 3. Space Jobs Grow Even As SpaceX Stock Cools  
  

SpaceX’s stock price has recently dropped, but the number of jobs in the space industry is still growing. Companies working on rockets, satellites, and space travel are **hiring** many new workers. This is good news for people who want to work in science and technology. The space economy is becoming an important part of the U.S. job market. Experts say the demand for skilled workers will continue to increase as space projects expand. This shows that even if a company’s stock falls, the industry itself can still be strong and have many opportunities.
