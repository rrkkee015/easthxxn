## 1. Severe Storms Forecast for Central U.S. After Tornadoes and Flooding ![Article 1](/images/news/2026-04-18-1.png)

The central United States is facing more **severe storms** after days of dangerous tornadoes and flooding. Weather experts say the storms could cause strong winds and heavy rain in many states. People living in these areas should be ready and **stay alert** for emergency warnings. Storms like this can damage homes and roads, and cause power outages. Officials are working to help communities prepare and respond quickly. This weather is a reminder of the power of nature and the importance of safety. In the coming days, more updates will tell us how bad the storms might be and what to expect next.

## 2. Immigration Enforcement Harms U.S. Schools and Students ![Article 2](/images/news/2026-04-18-2.png)

New research shows that immigration enforcement is **harming schools** and students in the United States. Many students feel scared and worried because of immigration raids and arrests near their schools. This fear can make it hard for students to focus on learning or feel safe. Teachers and staff say that school communities are becoming less trusting and more stressed. Experts suggest that schools need better support to protect students and give them a calm place to learn. The issue is important because education helps all children succeed, no matter where they come from.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **harming schools** — 학교에 해를 끼치다  
>   _The new policy is **harming schools** by creating fear among students._  
> - **feel scared** — 두려움을 느끼다  
>   _Children **feel scared** when they hear about immigration arrests._  
> - **focus on learning** — 학습에 집중하다  
>   _It is hard to **focus on learning** when you are worried._  
> - **give them a calm place** — 차분한 장소를 제공하다  
>   _Schools should **give them a calm place** to study and grow._

## 3. Job Openings in Colorado Remain Steady This Spring ![Article 4](/images/news/2026-04-18-4.png)

Colorado’s job market is showing steady **job openings** this spring, according to recent reports. Many businesses are looking for workers in areas like healthcare, technology, and hospitality. Even though some industries face challenges, overall hiring is still happening. Job seekers should keep **an eye on** new opportunities and prepare well for interviews. Experts warn that the fast hiring boom seen earlier might slow down soon, but steady jobs are still available. This news is good for people looking for work and for the state’s economy.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job openings** — 일자리 공석  
>   _There are many **job openings** in the technology sector._  
> - **an eye on** — 주의를 기울이다  
>   _You should keep **an eye on** job listings to find the best fit._  
> - **hiring boom** — 채용 급증  
>   _Last year’s **hiring boom** helped many people find jobs quickly._  
> - **steady jobs** — 안정적인 일자리  
>   _Even in slow times, some industries offer **steady jobs**._

## 4. How AI Is Changing Silicon Valley and the Tech World ![Article 3](/images/news/2026-04-18-3.png)

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is **changing the world**, especially in Silicon Valley. This new technology can help solve hard problems and create new products. Many companies are investing in AI to improve their services and stay ahead in the market. However, AI also raises questions about jobs, privacy, and how we use technology. People in Silicon Valley are trying to understand both the good and bad sides of AI. What happens in this tech hub will likely affect the whole world in the next years.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **changing the world** — 세상을 바꾸다  
>   _AI has the power to **change the world** in many ways._  
> - **stay ahead** — 앞서 나가다  
>   _Tech companies try to **stay ahead** by using new technology._  
> - **raises questions** — 의문을 제기하다  
>   _AI **raises questions** about privacy and job security._  
> - **the good and bad sides** — 좋은 면과 나쁜 면  
>   _We must consider **the good and bad sides** of AI carefully._
