## 1. IRS Faces Challenges Over Audit Immunity for Trump Family  


The IRS is in a difficult position because the Trump family may have **audit immunity**. This means they might not have to go through tax audits like other people. The IRS usually checks tax returns to make sure everything is correct. But if some people have special protections, it could be seen as unfair to others. This issue is important because it affects trust in the tax system. Some government officials are worried about what this means for fairness. The IRS must decide how to handle this situation carefully. The next steps could change how audits are done in the future.

## 2. Immigrants’ Access to Health Coverage Explained  


Many immigrants in the U.S. face difficulties getting health coverage. Some have insurance through their jobs, but others do not. Government programs like Medicaid or Medicare often have rules that make it hard for immigrants to qualify. This is important because health coverage helps people get medical care when they need it. The article explains key facts about which immigrants can get coverage and under what conditions. Understanding these rules helps people know their options. Policymakers may use this information to improve health access for immigrants in the future.

## 4. Silicon Valley’s Tech Workers Face a “Brutal” New Reality  


Many tech workers in Silicon Valley are losing their jobs and feeling lost. The industry is changing fast, and some companies are cutting staff to save money. This is called a **brutal** reality for those who expected steady work. Even skilled workers find it hard to get new jobs now. The article shows how this affects people’s lives and the tech world. Some say this change will reshape the future of technology. Workers need to adapt quickly to new challenges in this tough job market.
