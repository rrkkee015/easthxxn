## 1. Strong Job Growth Surprises in April 

The United States added 115,000 new jobs in April, which was a **surprisingly strong** number. Many experts expected fewer jobs to be created because the economy has been uncertain. Despite this growth, the unemployment rate stayed low, meaning many people who want to work can find jobs. This news shows that parts of the job market are still doing well, even with some worries about inflation and other problems. However, some experts say the numbers might not tell the whole story. They want to look deeper to see if these new jobs are good quality and if they will continue. This is important because jobs affect people's lives and the whole economy.

## 2. Immigration Policy and Researchers’ Free Speech Concerns 

Some experts and groups say that new immigration policies under former President Trump **chill researchers' speech**. This means that these policies make some researchers afraid to speak freely or share their ideas about immigration. The groups sent legal papers called "amicus briefs" to support this claim in court. They argue that strict immigration rules can affect academic work and political discussions. This is important because free speech and research help people understand immigration and make better policies. The debate shows how immigration rules can affect not only people who move but also those who study immigration.

## 3. A Changing Job Market is Harder for Men 

A recent report shows that the job market is changing in a way that makes it harder for men to find work compared to before. Many men used to work in industries like manufacturing and construction. These jobs are now shrinking or changing because of new technology and global business. Women are working more in growing fields like health care and education. This shift means men may need to learn different skills or move to new types of jobs. It is a big change in how the job market works and affects families and society. Understanding this helps us prepare for the future of work.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
> - **job market** — 일자리 시장  
>   _The **job market** can change quickly._  
> - **shrinking industry** — 줄어드는 산업

> - **learn new skills** — 새로운 기술을 배우다

> - **future of work** — 일의 미래

## 4. Silicon Valley’s Political Shift Changes Tech Industry 

Silicon Valley, the center of America’s tech industry, is seeing a new political group called the “Silicon Valley Right.” This group supports more conservative ideas than the tech industry usually does. This shift is changing how companies think about politics and business. Some say it affects hiring, product ideas, and even how tech companies work with the government. This new political landscape may change the future of technology and innovation in the US. Watching these changes helps us understand the connection between politics and technology.
