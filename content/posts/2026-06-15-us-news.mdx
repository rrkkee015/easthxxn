## 1. U.S. and Iran Announce Cease-Fire Agreement to End Conflict  


The United States and Iran have agreed to a **cease-fire** after a period of conflict. This agreement means both countries will stop fighting and work toward peace. The announcement came from former President Trump, who said this deal could help reduce tensions in the Middle East. Many people hope this will prevent further violence and improve safety for civilians. However, experts say it will take time to see if both sides truly follow the agreement. This step is important because it may affect global politics and oil prices. The world is watching closely to see what happens next.

## 2. U.S. Immigration Policy Faces Scrutiny Over Abuse Concerns  


A recent hearing in the U.S. government focused on the **abuse** of immigration policies. Lawmakers want to understand how some people misuse the rules meant to protect immigrants. The hearing aims to find ways to fix problems and make the system fairer. Immigration is a big topic because it affects millions of people and the country’s economy. Some argue that current policies are too strict, while others say they are too easy to exploit. The government plans to study the issue carefully and may suggest new laws soon. This debate shows how complex immigration is in the U.S.

## 3. Why Many Americans Struggle to Find Jobs Despite Economic Growth  


Although the U.S. economy is adding jobs, many people still find it hard to get hired. Experts say this is a surprising problem because companies are looking for workers. One reason is that some jobs require special skills not everyone has. Another reason is that many people want better pay and working conditions. Rising prices for things like food and housing make job seekers more cautious. This **job market** situation creates frustration for both employers and workers. The government and businesses are trying to find solutions to help more people get good jobs.

## 4. Tech Industry’s Big Win in California’s Primary Election  


In California, the tech industry spent millions to support politicians in the recent primary election. This **influence** helps tech companies protect their interests and shape laws. Many voters worry that big tech has too much power in politics. The election results show that money can play a big role in who gets elected. Some people say this could affect privacy rules, workers’ rights, and innovation. The tech industry says it wants to help the economy grow and create jobs. This story reminds us how important it is to watch how industries affect democracy.

> ### 영어 표현 학습
