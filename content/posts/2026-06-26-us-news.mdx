## 1. Supreme Court Supports Trump on Immigration Policies  
  

The U.S. Supreme Court recently made important decisions about immigration. The court allowed the Trump administration to end protected status for Haitian and Syrian migrants. This means some migrants could lose special permission to stay in the U.S. The court also cleared the way for strict immigration policies to return. These policies affect how people can ask for asylum at the border. These rulings are very important because they change who can stay in the country and under what rules. Many people are watching closely to see how these changes will affect immigration in the future.

## 2. US Jobless Claims Fall as Economy Faces Challenges  
  

Last week, the number of people applying for jobless aid in the U.S. dropped to 215,000. This shows that layoffs remain low even though the economy has some problems. Many companies are still hiring or keeping their workers. However, some experts warn that the job market might have hidden problems. For example, some people are working fewer hours or not finding good jobs. The jobless claim numbers are an important sign of how healthy the economy is. People will watch these numbers closely to understand what may happen next with jobs and the economy.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **jobless aid** — 실업 수당  
>   _He applied for **jobless aid** after losing his job._  
> - **layoff** — 해고  
>   _The company announced a **layoff** of 100 workers._  
> - **job market** — 취업 시장  
>   _The **job market** is very competitive right now._  
> - **face challenges** — 어려움에 직면하다

## 3. Venezuela Earthquake Shows Urgent Warning for California  
  

A strong earthquake recently hit Venezuela. The destruction was very serious. Experts say this earthquake is a big **warning** for California. California is a place where earthquakes happen often, but many buildings and people may not be ready for a big one. Scientists want people to prepare more carefully for future earthquakes. This includes improving building safety and having emergency plans. The Venezuela earthquake shows what could happen if a big earthquake hits California without warning. Preparing now could save many lives later.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **urgent warning** — 긴급 경고  
>   _The weather service issued an **urgent warning** about the storm._  
> - **destruction** — 파괴, 피해  
>   _The hurricane caused a lot of **destruction** in the city._  
> - **prepare for** — 준비하다  
>   _We must **prepare for** emergencies like earthquakes._  
> - **big one** — 큰 지진 (일상 표현)  
>   _Scientists say a **big one** could hit California soon._

## 4. Tech Industry Wins Big in California Election  
  

In California, the tech industry had a big success in a recent primary election. They spent millions of dollars supporting candidates who favor technology businesses. These candidates won important positions that can affect laws and rules for tech companies. This victory could help the tech industry grow and face fewer restrictions. California is known as a major tech hub, so these results matter for the whole country. Many people see this as a sign of how powerful the tech industry is in politics today.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **win big** — 크게 이기다, 크게 성공하다

> - **primary election** — 예비 선거  
>   _Voters choose candidates in the **primary election**._  
> - **favor** — ~에 찬성하다, 지지하다
