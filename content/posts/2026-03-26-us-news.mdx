## 1. US Sends Troops to Middle East While Proposing Iran Peace Plan  
![Article 1](/images/news/2026-03-26-1.png)

The United States is taking an important step by sending troops to the Middle East. At the same time, the US government is sharing a **peace plan** to reduce tensions with Iran. This plan aims to encourage talks and avoid conflict in the region. Sending troops shows that the US is ready to protect its interests and allies if needed. However, many people worry this could increase the risk of war. The peace plan is a way to try to find a peaceful solution while keeping military options open. What happens next depends on how Iran and other countries respond to this plan. This move is important because it could affect global security and oil markets.

## 2. ICE Begins Checking IDs at Airport Security Lines  
![Article 2](/images/news/2026-03-26-2.png)

Some airports in the US now have ICE agents checking people’s IDs as they wait in security lines. This is a new step to find people who may be in the country without proper papers. The goal is to improve safety and enforce immigration laws. However, some travelers feel nervous or uncomfortable because this adds extra steps to airport security. The government says this action is necessary to protect the country. It also shows how immigration enforcement is changing and becoming more visible in everyday places. This could affect how people travel and feel about security in the future.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **checking IDs** — 신분증을 확인하는 것    
>   _Officers were **checking IDs** before allowing passengers to enter._  
> - **security lines** — 보안 검색 줄    
>   _There were long waits in the **security lines** at the airport._  
> - **enforce immigration laws** — 이민법을 집행하다    
>   _The government works hard to **enforce immigration laws** fairly._  
> - **feel uncomfortable** — 불편함을 느끼다

## 3. US Job Market Pessimism Grows as Worker Thriving Declines  
![Article 3](/images/news/2026-03-26-3.png)

Many workers in the US feel less happy and successful in their jobs compared to before. A new study shows that fewer people say they are “thriving” at work. At the same time, more workers are worried about the job market and future opportunities. This feeling is called “pessimism,” which means expecting bad or difficult times. The job market is changing, and some industries are shrinking while others grow. College graduates, in particular, are finding it harder to get good jobs right now. These trends may cause more people to look for new career paths or change jobs soon.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 취업 시장    
>   _The **job market** is very competitive this year._  
> - **thriving at work** — 직장에서 성공하고 행복한 상태    
>   _She is **thriving at work** because she loves her job._  
> - **pessimism** — 비관주의, 어두운 전망    
>   _There is growing **pessimism** about the economy._  
> - **career paths** — 경력 경로    
>   _Many young people are exploring new **career paths**._

## 4. US and China Have Mixed Views on AI but Could Collaborate  
![Article 4](/images/news/2026-03-26-4.png)

The relationship between the US and China in artificial intelligence (AI) is complicated. A Silicon Valley tech founder says both countries are **disjointed** in how they think about AI development. This means their plans and rules do not match well. However, the founder believes there is still a chance for **collaboration** on AI research. AI is very important for future technology and security. The US worries China might get ahead if laws or policies fail to keep up. Talks about AI happen alongside other issues like the war in Iran. This shows how technology and politics are connected today.

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **disjointed** — 연결되지 않은, 따로따로인    
>   _Their ideas about the project were **disjointed** and confusing._  
> - **collaboration** — 협력, 공동 작업    
>   _The two companies agreed on **collaboration** to develop new software._  
> - **get ahead** — 앞서 나가다, 선두가 되다    
>   _She worked hard to **get ahead** in her career._  
> - **keep up** — 따라잡다, 뒤처지지 않다    
>   _It’s hard to **keep up** with all the new technology._
