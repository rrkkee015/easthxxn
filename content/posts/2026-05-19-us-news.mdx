## 1. Hate Crime at San Diego Islamic Center Shocks Community  
  

Last week, three people were killed at an Islamic center in San Diego. Police believe this was a **suspected hate crime**, meaning the attack was motivated by hate against a certain group. This terrible event has made many people feel unsafe and worried about hate crimes in the US. The community is coming together to support the victims’ families and call for peace. Authorities are still investigating to find out more about the attackers and their reasons. This story shows the importance of fighting hate and protecting all people from violence. It also reminds us to be kind and respectful to others, even if they are different from us.

## 2. What We Know About Children of Detained Immigrants  
  

The US government has detained about 400,000 immigrants recently. Many of these immigrants have children, but not much is known about what happens to those children. Some live with family members, while others may be in special care centers. This is a big concern because children need safety and support. Experts say it is important to build a system that is both **credible and humane** when dealing with immigration. The government is working on plans to improve how they help these families and protect children’s rights. This issue is important because it affects many young lives and the future of immigration policy in the US.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **detain** — 구금하다, 붙들다  
>   _The government **detained** many immigrants at the border._  
> - **credible and humane** — 신뢰할 수 있고 인간적인  
>   _We need a **credible and humane** way to treat immigrants._  
> - **concern** — 걱정, 우려  
>   _The safety of children is a major **concern** for everyone._

## 3. Why the Job Market Is Tough Right Now  
  

Many people are finding it hard to get good jobs in the US today. Experts have shared seven charts that explain this problem. One reason is that the economy is uncertain, which means businesses are not sure about their future plans. Also, some industries are shrinking, so there are fewer jobs available. College graduates are especially facing a **harsh job market** with lots of competition. This makes it difficult for new workers to find work they want. Understanding these trends helps people prepare better and look for jobs in growing fields. The government and companies may also need to create new programs to help workers during this hard time.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **job market** — 구직 시장, 취업 시장  
>   _The **job market** is very competitive for recent graduates._  
> - **harsh** — 혹독한, 어려운  
>   _Many students face a **harsh** job market after college._  
> - **uncertain** — 불확실한, 확실하지 않은  
>   _Economic conditions are **uncertain** right now._

## 4. The Rise of the “Silicon Valley Right” in Tech Politics  
  

In recent years, some technology leaders in Silicon Valley have started supporting more conservative political ideas. This group is called the “Silicon Valley Right.” They want to change the tech industry’s focus and policies. This new movement is **reshaping the tech landscape** by influencing how companies think about government, free speech, and innovation. It is surprising because Silicon Valley was known for more liberal ideas before. This change could affect how technology grows in the US and how it competes globally. Experts are watching closely to see what will happen next in this important industry.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **reshaping the tech landscape** — 기술 분야의 모습을 재구성하다  
>   _This new policy is **reshaping the tech landscape** in many ways._  
> - **movement** — 운동, 움직임  
>   _The **movement** gained support from many tech workers._  
> - **influence** — 영향력을 미치다  
>   _The group tries to **influence** government decisions._
