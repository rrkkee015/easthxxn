## 1. Federal Court Blocks Pause on Legal Immigration for Many Countries 

A federal court has **blocked** a government order that tried to **pause** legal immigration for nationals from 39 countries. This means people from these countries can continue applying for visas and enter the US legally. The pause was meant to limit immigration but judges said it was too broad and unfair. This decision is important because it affects thousands of immigrants waiting to come to the US for work, family, or safety. The government may try to appeal the ruling, but for now, the legal immigration process will continue. This shows how courts can **rein in** government actions that go too far. It also gives hope to many families and workers affected by the pause.

## 2. Why the US Job Market Is Stronger Than Expected 

The US job market is doing much better than many economists thought it would. More people are getting jobs, and companies are hiring faster than expected. This is surprising because some predicted slower growth due to high prices and other problems. However, wages are rising slowly, and some workers feel frustrated because the cost of living is still high. Experts say the strong job market is good news for the economy but also brings challenges like inflation. Many Americans hope this trend will continue and lead to better opportunities. Watching how the job market changes will help understand the US economy’s future.

## 4. Judge Throws Out Trump’s $100,000 Fee on H-1B Visas for Skilled Workers 

A federal judge has **thrown out** the Trump administration’s rule that charged a $100,000 fee for H-1B visas. These visas are for highly skilled workers, often from other countries, who work in tech, hospitals, and universities. The fee made it much harder and more expensive for companies to hire these workers. The judge said the fee was unfair and stopped it from being used. This decision is important because it helps keep a path open for talented immigrants to work in the US. Many tech companies welcomed the ruling and said it will help them find the workers they need.  

> ### 영어 표현 학습  
> - **throw out** — 기각하다, 무효로 하다  
>   _The court decided to **throw out** the new visa fee._  
> - **highly skilled** — 고도로 숙련된  
>   _The H-1B visa is for **highly skilled** workers._  
> - **pathway** — 경로, 방법  
>   _This visa is a major **pathway** for immigrants._  
> - **welcome** — 환영하다  
>   _Tech companies **welcome** the judge’s decision._
