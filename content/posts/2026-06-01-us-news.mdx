## 4. Silicon Valley Workers Struggle with Long Work Hours to Compete with China 

A former Google CEO warned that tech workers in the US may lose their **work-life balance** because they try to compete with China’s long 12-hour workdays. In many Chinese companies, employees work very long hours, which can be hard to match. This pressure makes US tech workers work late and feel tired. The CEO says it is not healthy to work so much, and companies should find better ways to manage work. This story shows the challenges in the global tech industry and how work culture affects people’s lives.
